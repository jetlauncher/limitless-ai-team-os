## 3 recommendations for next week

1. **Fix reach before optimizing content.** With 8–39 impressions/post, X distribution is the ceiling — not theme choice. Next week's lever: post consistency at the same slots, reply to 5–10 relevant accounts/day to prime the algorithm, and run at least 1 X thread (threads carry more impressions). Theme tuning is second-order until reach grows past ~few-hundred impressions/post.
2. **Lean owner-facing reframes over news framing.** Every engaged post used "you / your team / the CEO" + a clean reframe (Question Swap, Direct Address, Contrarian). Every flop used announcement/news framing ("X published…this week", "X is inside Excel now"). Mine Oracle (hourly shortform) harder for personal-reframe angles; treat Signal news items as raw material to be rewritten into an owner POV, not posted as-is.
3. **Add a reply-trigger / concrete close to short aphorism posts.** The 0-ER TH posts (X-6, etc.) were sharp one-liners with nothing to act on. Append a question or a "reply with X" to give the algorithm an engagement signal to work with.
