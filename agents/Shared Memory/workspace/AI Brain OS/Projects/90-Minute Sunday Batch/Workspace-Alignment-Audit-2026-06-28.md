## Proposed 90-minute Sunday workflow

### Inputs needed before starting

- Last 1–3 Plaud summaries, voice notes, or teaching notes.
- Jet brand/content pillars.
- One audience for the month.
- One offer/CTA for the month.
- One distribution map: Threads, IG, LinkedIn, X, carousel, email, reel.

### 0–10 min — Direction lock

Choose:
1. Main audience pain this month.
2. Offer/CTA.
3. 3 recurring themes.
4. 2 formats to emphasize.
5. One proof/story source.

Output:
- Monthly angle sentence.
- Content promise.
- Forbidden topics or tone.

### 10–25 min — Idea mining

Use sources in this order:
1. Plaud Insight Bank.
2. Recent Plaud summaries.
3. Jet content pillars.
4. Oracle/Signal recent ideas if needed.

Output:
- 30 raw post ideas.
- Label each by pillar and format.

### 25–40 min — Hook writing

For each idea, generate 2 hooks:
- direct pain hook
- reframe/provocation hook

Select the stronger one.

Output:
- 30 selected hooks.

### 40–60 min — Caption/body drafting

Use three caption templates:

1. **Business lesson caption**
   - insight
   - example
   - 3 bullets
   - CTA

2. **Founder proof caption**
   - scene
   - what changed
   - lesson
   - CTA

3. **Operator checklist caption**
   - mistake
   - checklist
   - action step
   - CTA

Output:
- 30 short captions or post bodies.

### 60–75 min — Repurpose map

Turn 30 posts into:
- 20 short posts / Threads / X ideas
- 4 carousels
- 4 reels/talking-head scripts
- 2 emails or longform posts

Output:
- monthly format calendar.

### 75–85 min — QA pass

Checklist:
- Does this sound like Jet?
- Is the business-owner problem clear?
- Is there a concrete example or math/proof?
- Is there a next action?
- Is the CTA aligned to the offer?
- Is anything generic AI slop?
- Is Thai/English routing correct?
- Does visual content have one idea per slide?

Output:
- 30 approved / revise / reject statuses.

### 85–90 min — Schedule/export handoff

Export:
- CSV/Notion table with date, platform, hook, caption, format, CTA, status.
- Optional: one carousel brief and one reel brief for the week.
