## Strategic update
Use **The Non-Technical Boss** as a new content lane inside the Sunday Content Engine. It gives Jet a sharper audience identity after the 100K YouTube milestone: the Thai business owner who does not want to become a programmer, but must learn to manage AI agents like a team.

## Positioning line
**You do not need to become technical. You need to become the boss of an AI team.**

## Pillars to add to the plan
1. **AI as employee, not app** — onboarding, JD, SOP, review, schedule.
2. **Trap diagnosis** — hiring, tool-hopping, prompt packs, manual admin, chaos automation.
3. **Peace as the outcome** — fewer decisions, fewer status checks, fewer midnight fixes.
4. **Payroll leverage math** — compare AI workflows to hires/agencies, not software subscriptions.
5. **Founder proof** — tie the 100K subscriber milestone to trust: the market wants translation for operators.
