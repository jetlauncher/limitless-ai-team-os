# Sunday Content Engine — Prompt Chain

## 1. Source digest prompt

Read the selected inputs and create a source digest with:
- 10 strongest ideas
- 10 strongest hooks
- 10 founder stories/examples
- 10 current AI news angles
- source citation for each

## 2. Pillar mapping prompt

Map every idea to one of Jet's pillars:
1. AI as payroll leverage
2. AI Team OS / agents as employees
3. Founder receipts / live demos
4. Thai business owner translation
5. 10x/teamless business design
6. Content and sales operating systems
7. Founder mindset / faith undertone

## 3. Output generation prompt

Generate the required outputs:
- 7 carousel concepts
- 30 <=90s reel scripts from real news + Jet voice
- 12 YouTube ideas with title options + rough outline
- 21 English LinkedIn posts
- 21 Thai IG Threads posts
- 30 English X posts
- 15 English X thread candidates

Every item must include source, pillar, hook, CTA, and status.

## 4. Review prompt

Score each item 1–5 on:
- source grounding
- Jet voice
- business-owner relevance
- clarity
- novelty
- format fit

Revise low-scoring items once. Reject instead of forcing weak content.
