# The Non-Technical Boss

## Highlight
The Non-Technical Boss: Diagnose the trap, then prescribe the AI system.

## Why Now
AI tool fatigue has set in. The market is shifting from 'what tool to use' to 'what new rules should I play by.' A structural driver is the proliferation of AI agents (Claude Cowork, Mann, Codex) that act more like employees than apps, making the 'operating mechanism' and 'diagnosis' frames urgent for a creator who needs to explain a new reality to overwhelmed business owners. This is the moment to show that peace, not just productivity, is the ultimate unlock, as the audience is experiencing burnout from information overload and feature hype.

## Signals
- Primary Thesis: Diagnose the trap, then prescribe the system.
- Top Mechanism: Reframing a common belief as a hidden trap.
- Top Format: Structured framework (5 levels, 3 stages, etc.).
- Emotional Driver: Relief from complexity sold as peace of mind.
- Visual Trend: Live demos of AI 'employees' doing the work.
