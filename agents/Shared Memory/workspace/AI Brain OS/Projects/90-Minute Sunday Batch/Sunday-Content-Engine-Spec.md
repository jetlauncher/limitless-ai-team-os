# Sunday Content Engine — Product Spec

**Owner:** Kelly EA Work  
**Project:** 90-Minute Sunday Batch / Sunday Content Engine  
**Purpose:** Turn Jet's source signals into a monthly content package every Sunday.

## Inputs

1. **Jet's Plaud transcripts**
   - Source: `~/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/AI OS/Limitless Academy/Plaud Library/`
   - Best use: voice, lessons, frameworks, founder stories, course IP.

2. **Jet's YouTube videos**
   - Source: YouTube transcripts/summaries in Shared Memory sources and YouTube workflows.
   - Best use: longform → shortform, authority topics, title patterns, rough outlines.

3. **Saved posts on X + watched videos on YouTube**
   - Best use: taste/inspiration layer, niche performance signal, pattern mining.
   - Current status: requires reliable export/access path. Do not fabricate this input; mark missing when not available.

4. **Latest AI updates from labs**
   - Source: X Search / official lab announcements / Signal radar.
   - Best use: real-news shortform reels and English X/LinkedIn commentary.

## Required outputs per Sunday batch

| Output | Quantity | Language | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Value-packed carousel posts | 7 | Thai-first or bilingual | 10–20 high-value posts/slide ideas per carousel batch |
| Shortform reels | 30 | Mostly Thai/English mix | <=90 seconds each, based on real AI news + Jet voice |
| YouTube video ideas | 12 | Thai or English title options | Based on niche performance + Jet voice + rough outline |
| LinkedIn posts | 21 | English | Founder/operator authority |
| IG Threads posts | 21 | Thai | Thai business owner voice |
| Shortform X/Twitter posts | 30 | English | Single-post, sharp POV |
| X thread options | 15 | English | Candidate threads for Jet to choose from |

## Quality principles

- Ground every item in at least one source: Plaud / YouTube / saved post / watched video / lab update.
- Real-news reels must cite the news/source internally.
- Jet voice > generic AI marketing voice.
- Thai posts must sound like a Thai founder/operator, not translated English.
- English LinkedIn/X should sound like a serious founder building an AI operating system.
- Keep carousels value-packed, not decorative.
- Reels must be shootable by Jet without a video team.
