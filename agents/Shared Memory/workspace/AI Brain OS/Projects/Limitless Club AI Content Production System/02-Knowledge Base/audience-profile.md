# Audience Profile

## Primary audience

Thai business owners, executives, and entrepreneurs learning to apply AI for real business leverage.

## Segments

### 1. Beginner AI business owners

- Wants practical AI workflows, not technical jargon.
- Pain: feels behind, overwhelmed by tools, unsure what to implement first.
- Desired identity: modern owner who can use AI without becoming a developer.

### 2. Advanced operators / executives

- Already understands AI basics.
- Wants systems, agents, dashboards, automation, and measurable ROI.
- Pain: team inefficiency, scaling bottlenecks, too much manual management.
- Desired identity: teamless / 10x operator.

### 3. Cohort / workshop prospects

- Needs proof that AI can save time, increase revenue, or reduce hiring pressure.
- Responds to real cases, numbers, and implementation examples.

## Avoid attracting

Low-purchasing-power curiosity traffic that likes AI tricks but does not buy training, workshops, or premium offers.
