## Mission

Empower builders to create without limits — using AI as the lever — so they win at business without losing their family, their faith, or their soul.

## Master lens

Every content idea must answer:

> Does this move the reader from trapped to free — from body-bound to limitless?

If not, kill it or reframe it.

## Required content filter

Before publishing, ask:

1. Is this based on something Jet built, tested, taught, broke, measured, or lived?
2. Does it move the reader from trapped to free?
3. Does it connect AI/business leverage to a better life, not just more output?
4. Does it preserve Jet’s faith/family/operator identity where relevant?
5. Are we using receipts, examples, numbers, screenshots, or story proof where possible?

If no, revise.
