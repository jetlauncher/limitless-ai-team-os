# P.A.C.T. Framework

Every short-form script and clip must score at least **16/20**.

## P — Provocative hook /5

Does the first 0–3 seconds stop a busy Thai business owner mid-scroll?

## A — Actionable insight /5

Is there one takeaway the viewer can use today?

## C — Contained narrative /5

Can the viewer understand the clip with zero outside context?

## T — Transformational identity /5

Does it speak to who the viewer wants to become?

## Scoring rule

- 18–20: Ship first.
- 16–17: Ship after light polish.
- 13–15: Rewrite.
- <13: Kill or re-angle.
