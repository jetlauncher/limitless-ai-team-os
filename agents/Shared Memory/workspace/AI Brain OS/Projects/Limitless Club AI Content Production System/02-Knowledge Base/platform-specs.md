# Platform Specs

## Instagram Reels

- 30–60 sec.
- Thai.
- 4:5 preferred for feed (1080×1350) or 9:16 when needed.
- Keep important text inside 1:1 safe zone.
- Dark/black background, white text, gold accent `#E8B84B`.
- Strong cover frame.

## TikTok

- 21–60 sec.
- Faster pacing, raw/native feel.
- Harder hook.
- Trend sound optional.

## YouTube Shorts

- 30–59 sec.
- Clear verbal/text link to full YouTube video.

## Facebook Reels

- 30–60 sec.
- Thai.
- Slightly clearer captions for older demographic.
- Avoid baiting low-purchase-power segments.

## X

- Text-first. Clip supports the post/thread.
- Jedi finalizes and posts in his own voice.

## LinkedIn

- English professional narrative.
- Story → insight → discussion prompt.
