# Brand Alignment Checklist

Use before writing, publishing, building, or proposing anything for Jedi.

## Source of truth

`02-Knowledge Base/jedi-personal-brand-strategy.md`

## The 7 checks

1. **Proof-led** — Is this based on what Jet built, tested, taught, broke, measured, or lived?
2. **Trapped → Free** — Does it move the reader from body-bound/calendar-owned to limitless/freer?
3. **AI as lever** — Does AI serve business/life leverage, not tool hype?
4. **Family/faith/soul** — Does it preserve the mission: win without losing family, faith, or soul?
5. **Operator receipts** — Are there real examples, screenshots, workflows, numbers, scars, or student/customer proof?
6. **Freedom architecture** — Does this reduce dependency on Jet’s body/calendar instead of increasing it?
7. **Clear pillar** — Does it fit AI-First Business, Life Design & Leverage, or Leadership & Fatherhood?

## Kill/reframe if

- It is generic AI trend commentary.
- It is lifestyle content with no proof or system connection.
- It increases live-delivery dependence without a leverage path.
- It does not ladder to freedom, family, faith, business leverage, or proof-led transformation.
