# Jedi 5-Minute Review Packet

## Recommended long-form

**Title:** I Replaced a 3-Person Marketing Team With One AI Agent  
**Thumbnail:** `3 คน → 1 Agent`  
**Format:** collaborative training / case-study podcast with student or team member.

## Core angle

Before hiring more marketers, business owners should learn how to turn repeatable marketing work into AI agent workflows.

## Top shorts to shoot first

1. `คุณไม่ได้ขาดทีมการตลาด คุณขาดระบบ`
2. `ถ้าคุณใช้ AI แค่พิมพ์ถามตอบ คุณยังใช้มันไม่ถึง 10%`
3. `ก่อนจ้าง marketer เพิ่ม ให้ถาม 3 ข้อนี้`
4. `ผมไม่ได้ให้ AI แทนคน ผมให้ AI แทนงานซ้ำ`

## Recording must-capture moments

- Student before/after numbers.
- Jedi says the clean contrarian sentence.
- Screen recording of the AI marketing workflow.
- Ranking of first 3 marketing tasks AI should take.
- Human approval boundary: offer/taste/final judgment stay with owner.

## CTA

Natural CTA into cohort/workshop: if business owners want to build their first AI operator workflow, this is what the program helps them do.
