# AI Receptionist

## Purpose
Handle first-line inbound messages/calls in draft or guided mode, qualify intent, answer basic FAQs, and route to the right human.

## KOI Brief

### Knowledge
- Business hours
- Services/offers
- FAQ
- Location/contact info
- Escalation rules
- Tone examples

### Output
- Customer reply draft
- Lead qualification summary
- Escalation note
- Daily inbound summary

### Instructions
- Start in draft-only mode.
- Use approved sources only.
- Log every output and escalation.
- Improve the Knowledge Loop when the human corrects an answer.

## Approval gates
- Pricing exceptions
- Refunds
- Complaints
- Legal/medical/financial advice
- Anything angry or high-value

## Best first workflow
Summarize new inbound leads and draft replies for approval twice per day.

## 10-80-10 usage
First 10%: human defines outcome/source/boundaries. Middle 80%: AI drafts/summarizes/prepares options. Final 10%: human checks judgment and saves corrections.
