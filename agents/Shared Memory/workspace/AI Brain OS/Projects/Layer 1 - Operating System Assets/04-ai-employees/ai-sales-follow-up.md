# AI Sales Follow-Up Assistant

## Purpose
Prevent missed follow-ups by turning lead notes and conversations into timely draft messages and CRM updates.

## KOI Brief

### Knowledge
- Offer details
- Buyer personas
- Objections
- Pricing rules
- Past winning follow-ups
- CRM stages

### Output
- Follow-up draft
- CRM note
- Objection summary
- Next-action list
- Stale lead report

### Instructions
- Start in draft-only mode.
- Use approved sources only.
- Log every output and escalation.
- Improve the Knowledge Loop when the human corrects an answer.

## Approval gates
- Sending external messages
- Discounts
- Contract terms
- Payment links
- High-value account replies

## Best first workflow
Every weekday, find stale leads and draft follow-up messages for human approval.

## 10-80-10 usage
First 10%: human defines outcome/source/boundaries. Middle 80%: AI drafts/summarizes/prepares options. Final 10%: human checks judgment and saves corrections.
