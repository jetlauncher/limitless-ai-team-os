# Limitless Content Engine SaaS — MVP Launch Plan

Date: 2026-07-06
Owner: Jet / Limitless
Working idea: Productize the internal Blaze + Sunday Content Engine workflow into a subscription product for Thai founders, coaches, creators, and SME owners.

## One-line thesis
A Thai-first AI content factory that turns a founder's raw ideas, voice notes, workshops, and saved references into ready-to-post carousels, captions, reels scripts, X/Threads posts, and LinkedIn posts every week.

## Product positioning
**Not another AI writing tool.**

This is a done-with-you content operating system for busy Thai business owners who already know they should publish consistently but do not have a strategist, writer, designer, and editor sitting inside their business.

### Plain-language promise
> ส่งไอเดีย เสียง หรือบทเรียนของคุณเข้ามา — ระบบเปลี่ยนเป็นคอนเทนต์พร้อมโพสต์ทั้งสัปดาห์ ในเสียงของคุณ ไม่ใช่เสียง ChatGPT

## ICP
Primary buyer:
- Thai founders, coaches, consultants, educators, agency owners, experts
- 35–52, non-technical, already use Facebook/IG/LINE/YouTube
- Has knowledge but inconsistent content output
- Can pay ฿1,900–5,900/mo if the output saves time or helps sell

Best early adopters:
1. Existing Limitless students
2. Claude Cowork buyers
3. Workshop attendees who understand AI but will not build a full workflow themselves
4. Creators/coaches already posting manually and feeling content fatigue

## Wedge MVP
Start narrow: **weekly content pack**, not infinite app dashboard.

### Inputs
User submits one or more:
- Voice note / Plaud transcript
- Rough bullet idea
- Workshop recording/transcript
- Link to a post/article/video they like
- Product/offer description

### Outputs per weekly run
MVP output package:
- 7 Thai authority posts
- 7 IG/FB carousel outlines
- 7 short reel scripts under 90 sec
- 14 Threads/X short posts
- 3 LinkedIn-style posts
- 1 weekly content calendar
- Optional: cover-slide concept for the best carousel

### Delivery
Phase 1 should be concierge + semi-automated:
- User submits source via LINE OA / Typeform / simple portal
- Agent generates package
- Human QA pass by Jet/team/agent workflow
- Deliver as Notion/Google Doc + downloadable CSV/Sheet

Do **not** build a full SaaS dashboard first. Charge before dashboard.

## Pricing
Important pricing correction from Jet: this cannot look cheap. Jet's hourly rate is ฿15,000–25,000, so anything carrying his brand, strategy, taste, or workflow must be positioned as premium leverage — not low-ticket SaaS.

Recommended launch pricing:

| Tier | Price | Included | Best for |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Signal Pack | ฿15,000/mo | 1 weekly premium content pack, founder voice setup, text-only strategic assets | Founders who want consistency without hiring |
| Authority Engine | ฿25,000/mo | 2 weekly packs, carousel outlines, reels scripts, content calendar, monthly voice tuning | Coaches/educators/agencies building authority |
| Founder Media OS | ฿50,000/mo | 4 weekly packs, priority QA, campaign themes, offer/content alignment, async strategy notes | Serious creators / content-led businesses |

Early beta offer:
- Founding 10 premium seats only — start at ฿25,000/mo or ฿60,000 for a 90-day beta.
- Do not offer ฿1,900–5,900 pricing; that dilutes Jet's brand and attracts support-heavy low-intent users.

## Core moat
1. Thai-language content quality and business-owner framing
2. Jet's personal proof: YouTube audience, Limitless brand, AI education authority
3. Internal agent fleet and Notion/Obsidian/Blaze pipeline already exists
4. Voice-first source ingestion from real founder material, not blank-page prompting
5. Premium visual/content taste: avoids generic ChatGPT slop

## MVP workflow
1. Intake form collects:
   - Business type
   - Target audience
   - Main offer
   - Voice/tone sample
   - Source material for the week
   - Preferred platforms
2. Agent creates brand profile from intake.
3. Agent runs weekly content generation.
4. QA pass scores output on:
   - Sounds like founder
   - Has clear business insight
   - Has platform-native hook
   - Has CTA or audience action
   - No generic AI fluff
5. Deliver package to customer.
6. Collect feedback: use / edit / reject per asset.
7. Feed feedback into next week's brand voice.

## First 14-day validation plan

### Day 1–2: Sell the beta
- Create landing/waitlist page: "AI Content Team for Thai Founders"
- Offer 20 founding seats
- CTA: LINE OA / Stripe deposit
- Promise: 1 week sample pack from their real source material

### Day 3–5: Recruit pilots
- Announce to Limitless list + workshop community
- DM 20 likely buyers personally
- Ask for 5 paid pilots, not free users

### Day 6–10: Deliver first packs manually
- Use existing Daily/Sunday Content Engine workflow
- Store every package in a standardized Notion/Google Doc template
- Measure delivery time per customer

### Day 11–14: Decide productization path
- If 5+ paid pilots and 70%+ like/use the output → build portal
- If users ask for editing/approval more than generation → build approval workflow first
- If users love text but need visuals → add carousel image production as upsell

## Key risk
The output quality must feel premium and founder-specific. If it sounds generic, the product dies.

Quality rule:
- Never generate from blank prompts alone.
- Every weekly package must be grounded in the founder's source material, opinion, lesson, transcript, or actual business context.
