# Identity Setup Prompt

Copy this into an AI chat to create `USER.md`, `SOUL.md`, and `IDENTITY.md`.

```text
Interview me to build my AI Brain identity files.

Goal: create three markdown files:
1. USER.md — who I am, how I think, my roles, goals, constraints, communication style, values, and frameworks.
2. SOUL.md — how my AI assistant should behave, speak, challenge, decide, and protect me.
3. IDENTITY.md — who this assistant is, what it owns, what it does not own, and when it should escalate.

Instructions:
- Ask me 10–15 focused questions first.
- Ask one question group at a time.
- Prefer practical questions over abstract personality questions.
- Capture my exact language when I say something important.
- After the interview, draft all three files in clean markdown.
- Do not include secrets, passwords, API keys, private tokens, or temporary tasks.
- Mark uncertain assumptions as: Needs human review.

Start with questions about:
- my role and current priorities
- how I prefer communication
- decisions I make repeatedly
- frameworks I use
- people and projects I care about
- what I want AI to do for me
- what AI must never do without approval
```
