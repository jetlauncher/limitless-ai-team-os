## 3. Hook System — The First 3 Seconds

CK's hooks follow **5 distinct patterns**. This is the most important section.

### Pattern 1: The Absolute Statement
> "The grass is greener where you water it"
> "Never take advice from those who never took risk"
> "Always pay debt first"
> "The only way to gain permanent stability is to be the best at what you do"

**Structure:** [Absolute word] + [contrarian truth]
**Why it works:** Stops the scroll because it sounds like a law of physics, not an opinion.

### Pattern 2: The "Two Types" Frame
> "Two types of people you should NEVER take advice from"
> "Business owner vs entrepreneur"

**Structure:** "Two types of [X]" or "[A] vs [B]"
**Why it works:** Creates instant tribal identity — the viewer MUST know which type they are.

### Pattern 3: The Question Provocation
> "เคยอยากเลิกทำ Fastwork?" (Ever wanted to quit Fastwork?)
> "What means rich for you?"

**Structure:** Ask a question that the viewer has secretly asked themselves
**Why it works:** Parasitic engagement — they answer in their head before you give yours.

### Pattern 4: The Named-Entity Drop
> "A great method from Elon"
> "Trust ChatGPT ❌ Trust CK-GPT ✓"
> "S-curve ใหม่ = AI"

**Structure:** Name a famous person, brand, or trend → then give your take
**Why it works:** Borrows authority from the named entity; algorithm loves recognizable names.

### Pattern 5: The Story Tease
> "The day I died, it will be well documented that I've never worked a day in my life"
> "I still get brief today: 'CK how can I make a viral content...'"

**Structure:** First-person narrative hook that implies a bigger story
**Why it works:** Curiosity gap — the viewer needs to hear the full story.

### Hook length
CK's hooks are almost always **3-7 words**. Never longer. The entire hook lands in the first 2 seconds.

## 4. Visual Style & Format

### 4.1 Primary Formats

| Format | Description | % of posts |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Walking + Talking** | CK walking outdoors (street, park, office corridor) speaking to camera | 40% |
| **Sitting + Talking** | Seated, usually in a studio, office, or cafe setting | 20% |
| **Podcast Clip** | Excerpt from his podcast, captioned, well-produced | 15% |
| **Event/Speaking** | Onstage at conferences, panels, interviews | 10% |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | Team, office life, transformations, collabs | 10% |
| **Trend/Meme** | Music overlays, transitions, humor | 5% |

### 4.2 Visual Signature

- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred. Golden hour, street lamps, office fluorescents. Rarely uses ring lights.
- **Background:** Always changing — streets, parks, offices, events, studios. Never a plain wall.
- **Wardrobe:** Casual-premium. T-shirts, polo shirts, occasionally blazer over tee. Never overdressed.
- **Camera:** Handheld or phone (vertical 9:16). Slight shake = authenticity. Tripod only for podcast.
- **Movement:** He's almost always in motion. Walking, gesturing, shifting weight. Static shots are rare.
- **Text overlays:** Minimal. Sometimes a single keyword or phrase on screen. Never full subtitles burned in-video (Instagram auto-captions used).
- **B-roll:** Minimal. Mostly single-take talking head. When B-roll appears, it's context (office, team, product).
- **Color grade:** Natural, slightly warm. No heavy filters or presets. Looks like real life, not a commercial.

### 4.3 Audio

- **Speaking voice:** Calm, measured, slightly slow. Never rushed or high-energy.
- **Tone:** Confident, philosophical, slightly melancholic. Like a mentor who's been through it.
- **Language:** English primarily, with Thai mixed in. Sometimes full Thai, sometimes full English.
- **Music:** Very subtle, often none. When music appears, it's ambient/mood — never trending audio for the sake of it.
- **No sound effects** — no swooshes, dings, or transitions sounds.

## 5. Script Structure — The 4-Part Formula

Almost every CK reel follows this structure:

### Part 1: The Hook (0-3 sec)
- Absolute statement, question, or provocation
- 3-7 words
- Delivered while walking or in mid-action

### Part 2: The Setup (3-10 sec)
- Context: why this matters
- Usually a personal story or reference
- "I learned this when..." / "People always ask me..."

### Part 3: The Payoff (10-30 sec)
- The core insight
- 1-2 sentences max
- Phrased as a universal principle, not a tip
- "The more you learn and the more you do, the luckier you get in life."

### Part 4: The Close (last 3-5 sec)
- A short, punchy line that restates the hook
- Or: a forward-looking statement ("And that's why...")
- Never: "Follow for more" or "Like and subscribe"
- The close is quiet, confident — like the end of a quote

### Example breakdown: "Never take advice from those who never took risk"

| Section | Words | Timing |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Hook | "Never take advice from those who never took risk" | 0-3s |
| Setup | "There are two types of people who give advice. Those who have built something, and those who haven't." | 3-10s |
| Payoff | "If you're building a business, don't take advice from someone who's never built one. Their risk tolerance is zero. Their advice will keep you small." | 10-25s |
| Close | "Don't be too tied to what's safe." | 25-28s |

### Key rule
**One idea per reel.** CK never packs 3 tips into one video. One truth, one story, one reel.

## 6. Caption Strategy

CK's captions are **minimal and powerful**.

### Caption patterns:
1. **The hook repeated** — Caption = the spoken hook text, verbatim
2. **One-line elaboration** — Hook + one extra sentence
3. **Hashtag cluster** — 3-5 relevant hashtags, no spam
4. **Call to action (rare)** — "Full podcast on YouTube" or "Save this reel"

### What CK NEVER does:
- Long essay captions (he saves those for Substack/newsletter)
- Emoji spam
- "Tag a friend who needs to see this"
- Engagement-bait questions ("Comment YES if you agree")
- More than 5 hashtags

### Sample captions:
- "The grass is greener where you water it" + 3 hashtags
- "3 recommendations for 2026" + no extra text
- "Always pay debt first!" + 2 hashtags
- "Trust ChatGPT ❌ Trust CK-GPT ✓" + no extra text

### Hashtag rotation:
`#mindset #success #wealth #selfimprovement #entrepreneur #business #AI #fastwork #ckfastwork #CKCheong`

- **The patriot with a global mind** — "I want to make Thailand better" but thinks globally
- **The quiet warrior** — Calm delivery, deep conviction, no shouting

`Calm. Measured. Philosophical. Contrarian. Vulnerable. Confident. Patriotic. Global.`

1. **Proof of identity** — He's a CEO, so his opinions carry weight

## 12. Summary — The CK Cheong Short-Form Code

```
1. One truth per reel. Never pack multiple tips.
2. Hook in 3-7 words, first 2 seconds.
3. Walk and talk. Never static behind a desk.
4. Natural light. No filters. Real life.
5. Calm, slow, confident delivery.
6. Personal story → universal principle.
7. Captions: hook text + 3 hashtags. Nothing more.
```
