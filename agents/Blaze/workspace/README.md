## Where things go
- `Memory/` — durable agent-specific facts, preferences, systems, watchlists, and conventions.
- `Daily/` — dated working notes, handoffs, and near-term context.
- `Protocols/` — repeatable workflows and operating procedures.
- `Scratchpad/` — temporary notes that should later be promoted or deleted.
- `Ideas/` — exploratory ideas that are not yet commitments.
