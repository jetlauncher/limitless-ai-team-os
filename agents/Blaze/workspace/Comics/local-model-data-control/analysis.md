## Core Point to Illustrate

Local AI models are not just about saving subscription money. They are about **control**: where sensitive business data lives, who sees it, and which workflows should stay inside the company.

## Founder Implication

Most founders hear “local model” and think: cheap/free AI. The sharper insight is:

- Cloud AI is convenient and powerful.
- Local AI gives privacy, control, and independence.
- The strategic question is not “which model is smartest?”
- The better question is “which data should never leave our building?”

## Best Comic Angle

**Title direction:** “AI ไม่ได้มีแค่บน Cloud” / “ข้อมูลบางอย่างไม่ควรขึ้น Cloud”

A founder is about to pour customer data into a big cloud-shaped AI. A mentor/IT operator stops him and shows two doors: “Cloud” and “Local”. Cloud is fast and bright. Local is a secure office vault with a small AI brain running inside. The aha moment: use cloud for public/general tasks, use local for sensitive internal data.

## Visual Symbol System

- **Cloud AI:** large floating cloud with plug/cable, bright but external.
- **Sensitive data:** folder labeled “ลูกค้า / ยอดขาย / ไอเดียใหม่”.
- **Local model:** small AI brain inside a locked laptop or vault.
- **Decision rule:** split-path sign: “Public → Cloud” and “Private → Local”.
