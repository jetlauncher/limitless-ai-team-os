## Four-Panel 起承转合 Structure

### Panel 1 — 起 / Setup

**Scene:** Founder at a messy desk late at night, surrounded by floating chat bubbles and unfinished content drafts.

**Dialogue:**
Founder: “ทำไมใช้ AI แล้วยังเหนื่อยเหมือนเดิม?”

**Visual symbol:** tangled cables and chaotic chat bubbles.

### Panel 2 — 承 / Development

**Scene:** Founder draws a small org chart on a whiteboard: Signal, Blaze, Higgsfield. The agents appear as tiny workers at tiny desks.

**Dialogue:**
Founder: “หรือจริงๆ เราไม่ได้ต้องการผู้ช่วย…”

**Visual symbol:** small bronze labels above each agent role.

### Panel 3 — 转 / Turn / Aha

**Scene:** The tiny AI team starts working in a clean command room while founder sleeps. Signal watches market radar, Blaze drafts content, Higgsfield creates visuals. A bronze Approval Gate blocks the “Publish” button.

**Dialogue / label:**
Agent label: “ทำได้หมด… แต่ยังไม่โพสต์”
Gate label: “Approve?”

**Visual symbol:** bronze Approval Gate is the strongest accent element.

### Panel 4 — 合 / Conclusion

**Scene:** Morning. Founder returns to a clean dashboard showing 3 strong ideas, 2 visual mockups, and a big approve/revise/reject choice.

**Dialogue:**
Founder: “อ๋อ… AI ไม่ได้แทนเรา แต่มันเตรียมการตัดสินใจให้เรา”

**Caption below comic:**
Founder AI OS = บทบาทชัด + ข้อมูลครบ + มีด่านอนุมัติ
