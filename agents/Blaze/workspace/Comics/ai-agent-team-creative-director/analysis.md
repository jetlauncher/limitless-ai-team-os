## Core Point to Illustrate

A founder should stop thinking of AI as one chatbot and start designing a small AI team with clear roles, boundaries, and approval gates.

The most visual version of the idea:

> “You don’t hire an AI assistant. You architect an AI operating system.”

## Market / Founder Implication

For Thai founders and operators, the leverage is not just faster content generation. The real shift is:

- AI can monitor, draft, design, and coordinate while the founder sleeps.
- The founder’s new job is not prompting harder. It is defining roles, context, guardrails, and review loops.
- Tools like Hermes + Higgsfield make creative production feel less like outsourcing one task and more like operating a 24/7 creative department.

## Best Comic Angle

**Title direction:** “The Night Shift Creative Team” / “ทีมครีเอทีฟที่ไม่เคยหลับ”

A busy founder leaves his desk at night. Tiny AI agents inside a dark editorial command room wake up:

- Signal watches the market radar.
- Blaze turns raw ideas into content concepts.
- Higgsfield builds beautiful ad/video variations.
- Que / Approval Gate prevents anything public from going live without the founder.

The founder returns in the morning and does not see a pile of random AI output. He sees a clean decision board: 3 strong concepts, visual mockups, and one “approve / revise / reject” checkpoint.
