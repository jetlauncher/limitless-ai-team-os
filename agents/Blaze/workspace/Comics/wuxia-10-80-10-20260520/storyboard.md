# Storyboard — The 10-80-10 Technique

## Format
Single premium 4:5 comic poster with one cinematic splash scene plus three inset panels.

## Page Structure

### Title Area
**THE 10-80-10 TECHNIQUE**
Subtitle: Direction. Execution. Judgment.

### Main Splash Scene
A calm founder-warrior stands on a cliff above a chaotic battlefield of tasks. Instead of charging into every fight, he channels three streams of golden qi into a floating strategy seal.

### Inset Panel 1 — 10% Direction
Founder draws a small precise brushstroke/sword arc. Label: “10% Direction”. Visual: a golden command seal forms.

### Inset Panel 2 — 80% Execution
The seal releases many disciplined agent-warriors / paper talismans / bronze spirits that handle the battlefield. Label: “80% Execution”. Visual: motion, speed lines, organized swarm.

### Inset Panel 3 — 10% Judgment
Founder returns with a final decisive sword stroke, cutting through noise and approving the result. Label: “10% Judgment”. Visual: clean impact ring, calm authority.
