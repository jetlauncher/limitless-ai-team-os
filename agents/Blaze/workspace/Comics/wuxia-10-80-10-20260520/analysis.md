# Wuxia Founder Comic — Analysis

## Topic
The founder who mastered 10-80-10: never fighting the whole battle himself.

## Business Lesson
Founders lose leverage when they try to personally execute every task. The stronger operating model is:
- 10% direction: define the mission, constraints, taste, and success criteria.
- 80% execution: let AI/team/systems do the heavy lift.
- 10% judgment: inspect, decide, refine, and own the final call.

## Visual Metaphor
A founder-warrior stops fighting every enemy alone. He learns the “10-80-10 technique”: one hand draws the strategy seal, the army of agents executes, then he returns for the final sword stroke of judgment.

## Audience
Founder/operators, SME owners, creators building AI-powered systems.
