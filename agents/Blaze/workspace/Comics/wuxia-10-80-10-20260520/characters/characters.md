# Characters

## Founder-Warrior
A modern Thai founder reimagined as a wuxia strategist. Mid-30s to early-40s, calm confident expression, black founder-style robe mixed with modern tailored jacket, bronze belt accent, clean hair moving in the wind. Carries a thin scholar-warrior sword and a brush-like command talisman. Presence is calm, premium, strategic, not aggressive.

## Agent-Spirits
Abstract AI/team execution figures represented as disciplined bronze-gold paper talismans, shadow warriors, and glowing task spirits. They are not scary or evil; they are focused, organized extensions of the founder's system.

## Chaos Tasks
Dark smoky task-demons and scattered scrolls representing random to-dos, meetings, content drafts, sales follow-ups, support tickets, and admin work. They should look chaotic but not horror-like.
