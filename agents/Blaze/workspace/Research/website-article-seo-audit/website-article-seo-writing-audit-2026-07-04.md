## Writing diagnosis using Limitless evaluator
- Current style often starts with broad generic openings like “ในยุคที่ AI...” rather than a concrete owner scene or tension.
- Many pieces explain tools, but do not make a strong strategic claim about how a Thai business owner should change operations.
- The best future articles should follow: **owner scene → sharp thesis → mechanism → workflow → risk/tradeoff → first 7-day action plan → CTA**.
- Target article score should be 80+ by the Limitless Writing & Thought Evaluator; current real articles roughly cluster around 55–75, while stubs are effectively <20.

## Recommended article structure
1. **H1 / Title**: Thai SEO keyword + business outcome. Keep <=70 chars when possible.
2. **Opening owner scene**: a concrete Thai SME/founder moment, not generic AI hype.
3. **Thesis**: one strong point of view: “AI is not a tool problem; it is an operating-system problem.”
4. **Mechanism**: explain how the tool/workflow actually creates leverage.
5. **Founder framework**: named model such as 10-80-10, CLEAR, KOI, Founder AI OS.
6. **Use cases**: 3–5 concrete business scenarios.
7. **Risks/tradeoffs**: what not to automate; governance/privacy/cost caveats.
8. **7-day implementation plan**: practical steps.
9. **FAQ block**: 3–5 search-intent questions.
10. **CTA**: LINE OA / program / relevant YouTube video / related internal articles.
