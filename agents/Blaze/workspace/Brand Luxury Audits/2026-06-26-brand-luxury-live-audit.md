## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Standardize the highlight cover row into a black/bronze/ivory icon system
- **Exact change:** Draft new highlight covers with charcoal `#17181A`, tiny ivory line icons, optional thin bronze ring, and short restrained labels: `Creative AI`, `Free`, `Corporate`, `Workshop`, `Classes`, `Proof`, `Events`. Remove emoji from the label display where possible, especially `เรียนรู้ฟรี😍` → `Free` or `เรียนฟรี`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Highlights are the first brand shelf under the bio. A unified, quiet row makes the profile feel curated like a premium media brand rather than a storage row of Stories.
- **Quick implementation note:** Design as drafts for Jet approval only. Keep label length short so the row scans elegantly on mobile.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Reduce emoji density in the bio preview
- **Exact change:** Draft a calmer first three lines for approval, e.g. `AI systems & growth strategy for 7–8 figure founders` / `1,000+ students trained` / `Limitless Club — practical AI for business operators`. If Thai-first: `AI Systems สำหรับเจ้าของธุรกิจ 7 หลัก+` / `สอนผู้ประกอบการแล้ว 1,000+ คน` / `Limitless Club | AI × Growth × Operations`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury profiles rely on authority, specificity, and restraint. Less emoji makes the same proof feel more expensive.
- **Quick implementation note:** This is a live profile edit; keep as a draft until Jet approves.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Apply a visible “premium cover rule” to pinned/top-grid strategic posts
- **Exact change:** For any pinned post or strategic carousel near the top grid, use the Midnight Luxe cover system: charcoal background, ivory serif headline, small `LIMITLESS CLUB` masthead, `@jeditrinupab` footer, one subtle bronze accent max. Avoid screenshot-heavy or yellow-caption covers for posts intended to build authority.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The first 9 tiles are the profile’s storefront. A few consistent editorial covers create instant brand memory even if Reels remain more casual.
- **Quick implementation note:** Do not change old posts now. Use the rule for future pinned/evergreen posts and any new flagship carousel.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Separate proof/event posts from luxury authority posts with a consistent badge
- **Exact change:** On event/class recap covers, add a small recurring badge such as `LIMITLESS CLASSROOM` or `LIVE WORKSHOP` in ivory/bronze, rather than large celebratory mixed typography. Keep group photos, but frame them with a black editorial border and quieter caption.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Proof photos are valuable, but they can look casual or crowded. A restrained badge turns social proof into a branded asset.
- **Quick implementation note:** Start with the next class recap. No retroactive edits needed.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Create a Reel cover fallback for casual talking-head clips
- **Exact change:** For selfie/talking-head Reels, choose a cover frame with Jet centered, then add a small dark title band with ivory Thai hook text and tiny `LIMITLESS CLUB`. Avoid yellow shadowed captions on the grid cover when the goal is premium association.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Casual video is authentic, but the grid cover needs editorial control. A simple fallback keeps Reels from breaking the storefront.
- **Quick implementation note:** Use only for selected strategic Reels; raw casual covers can remain for spontaneous posts.
- **Priority:** Medium
