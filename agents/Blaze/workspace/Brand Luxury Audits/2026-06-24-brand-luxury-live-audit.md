# Brand Luxury 1% Live Audit — Instagram — 2026-06-24

## Scope
- Focus surface: Instagram `instagram.com/jeditrinupab`
- Rotation rationale: 2026-06-23 audit was YouTube, so today rotates to Instagram.
- Live access: public logged-out Instagram view worked after closing the sign-up overlay. Inspected profile header, avatar, bio preview, highlights, and visible grid first impression.
- No public assets were edited, posted, scheduled, boosted, or changed.

## First-impression read
Jet’s Instagram has strong authority and social-proof signals: verified account, 92.6K followers, 6,343 posts, professional portrait avatar, business/AI positioning, student count, and visible workshop/event proof. The premium gap is not credibility; it is restraint and consistency. The profile currently feels energetic and high-volume: emoji-heavy bio preview, many mixed highlight cover styles, and a grid where event collages, talking-head Reels, English bold captions, Thai carousel typography, and casual smartphone shots all compete. The best luxury opportunity is to make the above-the-fold profile memory calmer: one bio rhythm, one highlight cover system, and more disciplined first-nine cover hierarchy.

## Visual observations
- **Avatar:** Strong and premium. Dark portrait, direct eye contact, black jacket, and clean crop support the expensive-founder signal. Keep as anchor.
- **Bio/header:** Name line reads `Jedi | AI Growth Coach & Business Consultant`; category `Entrepreneur`; bio preview uses 🚀 / 🏆 / 🔥 and says AI Coach for 7-figure+ business owners and 1,000+ students. Strong proof, but the emojis and truncated `... more` make it feel growth-coach/social-first rather than editorial premium.
- **Highlights:** Labels include `Creative AI`, `เรียนรู้ฟรี😍`, `Corp. Training`, `AI Workshop`, `บรรยากาศคลาส`, `Testimonials`, `Events`, plus more. The covers are mostly photos/screenshots with different lighting and density. This proves activity, but lacks a luxury brand shelf.
- **Grid first impression:** Strong proof stack: Limitless Club class photo, Jet in blazer, English Reel quote cover, midnight-style Thai carousel, talking-head AI Reel, hotel/classroom training clips. The mix proves momentum but has uneven cover discipline.
- **Typography/color:** The midnight carousel cover with small `LIMITLESS CLUB`, warm ivory, and restrained circle is closest to Midnight Luxe Editorial. Other covers use bright yellow subtitles, blue blocks, casual mobile lighting, and mixed fonts.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Replace highlight covers with a uniform “black seal” system
- **Exact change:** Create 7–9 matching highlight covers using `#17181A` background, warm ivory labels, one thin bronze ring/line, and small category text: `CREATIVE AI`, `FREE LESSONS`, `CORP TRAINING`, `WORKSHOP`, `CLASSROOM`, `TESTIMONIALS`, `EVENTS`, `SERVICES`, `STORY`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Highlights are the profile’s display shelf. Matching covers instantly make the account feel curated, not accumulated.
- **Quick implementation note:** Draft covers for approval first. Keep photo-based stories inside each highlight; only replace the cover image if Jet approves.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Edit the visible bio style from emoji-led proof to editorial proof
- **Exact change:** Draft a calmer bio version for approval:
  - `AI Operating Systems for 7–8F Founders`
  - `Limitless Club founder • 1,000+ students`
  - `Training / workshops / founder media`
  - `↓ Start here`
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury positioning leads with identity and category ownership, not urgency emojis. It still keeps the credibility numbers but makes the profile sound more expensive.
- **Quick implementation note:** Do not change live bio without approval. Test one Thai-forward version and one bilingual version before deciding.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Pin/sequence the top grid into a 3-post premium entry path
- **Exact change:** Make the first row tell one premium story: `What Limitless Club is` + `Jet authority portrait / founder thesis` + `student or workshop proof`. Avoid having one row split between event collage, portrait, and unrelated English quote cover.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** A premium profile behaves like a lobby: new visitors should understand category, authority, and proof in 3 seconds.
- **Quick implementation note:** If pinning is available, draft pin candidates only. If not, use the next 3 posts to create the row.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Add one small brand masthead to carousel/Reel covers
- **Exact change:** Use a consistent micro-header on future covers: `LIMITLESS CLUB` top-left, `@jeditrinupab` bottom-left, and a thin bronze metadata line. Keep it under 6% of the cover area.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Repeated small marks create premium memory without making content look like ads.
- **Quick implementation note:** Start with new carousels and authority Reels only; do not retrofit casual behind-the-scenes posts.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Separate “proof photos” from “editorial covers”
- **Exact change:** For workshop/class/event posts, use a designed cover slide first, then put the group photo as slide 2. Cover formula: quiet title, venue/client/category, one cropped proof image or no image.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** High-ticket brands use proof selectively. Group photos are valuable, but when every proof image becomes the cover, the grid feels operational rather than editorial.
- **Quick implementation note:** Use the class photo asset, but crop it smaller under a black editorial frame.
- **Priority:** Medium

## Today’s recommended 1% upgrade

Why this is the best move today:
- It upgrades the most visible permanent shelf under the bio without touching posts or algorithmic performance.
- It makes the profile immediately feel more curated, premium, and brand-owned.
- It is reversible and approval-friendly: Jet can review covers before any live edit.
