## First-impression read
Jet's Instagram already has strong authority signals: verified profile, 92.6K followers, 6,354 posts, premium dark studio avatar, visible AI/business positioning, and strong social proof from classes/events. The main luxury gap is restraint and systemization: emoji-heavy bio preview, mixed highlight cover language, and a grid that alternates between premium Limitless visuals, casual selfie Reels, group photos, and bright English/Thai text overlays. The account feels active and credible; the 1% upgrade is to make the top surface feel more curated and expensive before asking visitors to consume content.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Draft a calmer bio preview with proof but fewer emojis
- **Exact change:** Keep the authority promise, but replace the visible first lines with a more editorial version, e.g. `AI Systems for 7–8 Figure Founders` / `Limitless Club — AI, growth, and operator leverage` / `1,000+ students • Workshops • Consulting`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Premium profiles state category ownership with confidence. Removing the rocket/trophy/fire stack reduces hype and makes Jet feel more like a founder-media authority than a sales coach.
- **Quick implementation note:** Draft only for Jet approval. If Thai-first is preferred: `AI Systems สำหรับนักธุรกิจ 7–8 หลัก` / `Limitless Club — AI • Growth • Operator Leverage` / `ลูกศิษย์ 1,000+ คน • Workshop • Consulting`.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Create a unified Highlight cover system
- **Exact change:** Replace varied photo covers with a restrained Midnight Luxe set: charcoal background `#17181A`, warm ivory label, small bronze divider/icon, no emoji. Suggested labels: `START`, `FREE`, `WORKSHOP`, `CORP`, `WINS`, `PROOF`, `EVENTS`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Highlights are the profile's storefront shelves. A consistent cover system instantly makes the page feel curated, intentional, and expensive.
- **Quick implementation note:** Start with only the first 7 visible highlights so the above-fold row becomes clean. Keep the actual story content unchanged.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Give pinned/grid hero posts a consistent premium frame
- **Exact change:** For the top 3 pinned posts or next 3 strategic posts, use a shared frame: tiny `LIMITLESS CLUB` masthead, graphite/black base, warm ivory headline, bronze micro-accent, and consistent footer `@jeditrinupab`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The first grid currently reads as many independent moments. A recurring frame creates brand memory while still allowing Reels and social proof to perform.
- **Quick implementation note:** Do not redesign every past post. Apply the system to pinned posts or future flagship carousels only.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Rename one emoji-coded highlight label
- **Exact change:** Change `เรียนรู้ฟรี😍` to `FREE LESSONS`, `FREE AI`, or Thai minimalist `เรียนฟรี` with no emoji.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The emoji is friendly, but it lowers perceived price/premium value in the first highlight row. A calmer label makes the free content feel like a curated library, not a giveaway bin.
- **Quick implementation note:** This is a small live profile edit; keep as a draft for approval.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Add a quiet proof post template for workshop/class photos
- **Exact change:** When posting class group photos, add one subtle branded slide first or last: `LIMITLESS CLUB`, cohort/session name, date, and one proof line. Use ivory on charcoal with a bronze rule.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Social proof remains real, but the proof is packaged like an institution rather than a casual event album.
- **Quick implementation note:** Use only for future class/event posts. The photo itself can stay human; the branded card creates the premium wrapper.
- **Priority:** Medium

## Today’s recommended 1% upgrade
**Draft a unified Highlight cover system for the first 7 visible highlights.**

Why this is the best move today:
- It upgrades the above-fold Instagram storefront without touching content strategy.
- It is small, visual, and high-leverage: every profile visitor sees it before scrolling.
- It makes the account feel more like a premium founder-media brand while preserving Jet's active proof, classes, and Reels.
