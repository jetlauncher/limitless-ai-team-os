- Focus surface: YouTube `youtube.com/@jeditrinupab`
- Rotation rationale: latest accessible prior rotation was Instagram/YouTube alternating; 2026-06-22 audit file could not be read because macOS returned `Resource deadlock avoided`, so today defaults to YouTube as the higher-impact visible packaging surface.
- Live access: worked in public logged-out YouTube view. Inspected channel Home, Videos > Latest, and Videos > Popular.
- No public assets were edited, posted, scheduled, boosted, or changed.

## First-impression read
Jet’s YouTube has strong authority signals: 87.3K subscribers, 1.3K videos, a clean professional avatar, active AI/operator content, and several high-performing videos in the 40K–82K range. The luxury gap is mainly packaging discipline: the banner already gestures toward premium founder-media, but the right-side red Subscribe graphic, social icon strip, mixed event-photo collage, and thumbnail palette create a more creator/YouTube-growth look than a high-class media brand. Recent thumbnails are high-energy and clickable, but they use many colors, emojis/punctuation, product logos, arrows, shocked expressions, glow effects, and inconsistent title frames. The channel can become more expensive-looking without losing performance by standardizing one small premium metadata/masthead system.

## Visual observations
- **Banner:** black base and Jet portrait fit the Midnight Luxe direction, but the red `SUBSCRIBE` button graphic and many social icons cheapen the premium feel. The left/right event-photo collages prove scale, yet feel busy beside the main message.
- **Avatar:** strong, calm, expensive-looking; better than most visible assets. It should remain the anchor.
- **Homepage:** first visible row mixes bright orange/yellow AI thumbnails, grey comparison thumbnails, green glow, and red/orange AI clash treatment. The channel reads useful and active, but less restrained than the brand rule.
- **Latest thumbnails:** strong click intent, but first three thumbnails compete with one another through oversized type, orange/yellow highlights, multiple product marks, cartoon-like icons, percentage badge, arrows, and expressions.
- **Popular thumbnails:** best performers reveal a repeatable system: Jet portrait + clear problem + tool/context. However, the system is not yet visually branded as Limitless Club; it looks more like topic-by-topic YouTube packaging.
- **Titles/captions:** video titles are clear and practical, but formats alternate between Thai-only, English-only, `[Full Course]`, question-style, comparison-style, and dramatic claim-style. That can be fine editorially, but premium perception improves if major series have a controlled prefix taxonomy.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Replace the banner’s red Subscribe/social block with a quiet editorial brand plaque
- **Exact change:** In the banner’s right-center area, replace the red `SUBSCRIBE` button, cursor hand, and social icon strip with a restrained plaque:
  - `LIMITLESS CLUB`
  - `AI OPERATING SYSTEMS • FOUNDER MEDIA`
  - `@JEDITRINUPAB`
  Use charcoal/black base, warm ivory text `#E2D7C8`, and one thin bronze line `#94764A`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury brands do not ask loudly; they signal identity calmly. Removing the red YouTube-native callout reduces creator-noise and makes the channel feel like a premium media property rather than a subscriber-hunt page.
- **Quick implementation note:** Keep the same Jet portrait and main Thai headline if performance/recognition matters. Only simplify the right-side CTA zone first; no full rebrand needed.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Add a tiny Limitless Club masthead strip to all new AI thumbnails
- **Exact change:** Standardize a 5–7% top-left or bottom-left strip on future thumbnails: `LIMITLESS CLUB` / episode category / thin bronze rule. Keep it small enough not to hurt CTR.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Repetition creates brand memory. A restrained masthead gives the grid an editorial spine even when topics, tools, and faces change.
- **Quick implementation note:** Use `#17181A` rectangle, `#E2D7C8` text, and `#94764A` hairline. Apply only to new uploads for two weeks before retrofitting old winners.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Cap thumbnail accent colors to one loud color plus bronze/ivory
- **Exact change:** For each thumbnail, keep only one performance color from the topic asset (orange, cyan, green, or red), then pair it with black/graphite/ivory and a rare bronze accent. Avoid using orange + yellow + red + blue + green in the same frame.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Expensive visuals feel edited. Color restraint makes the channel look more intentional while preserving one high-contrast hook for YouTube scanning.
- **Quick implementation note:** Create a pre-export checklist: `1 loud color max`, `bronze only for hierarchy`, `no more than 2 product logos`, `no emoji unless concept-critical`.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Create a premium series taxonomy for titles and thumbnails
- **Exact change:** Use 3–4 recurring labels for high-value uploads, e.g.:
  - `AI Agent 101`
  - `Founder OS`
  - `Tool Test`
  - `Full Course`
  Show the same label in the thumbnail masthead and title prefix only for major videos.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Premium media feels curated, not random. A taxonomy makes the channel feel like a publication with departments.
- **Quick implementation note:** Keep the existing Thai performance titles after the label. Example draft: `Founder OS: วิธีประหยัด Token ใน Claude Cowork`.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Tone down “shock” punctuation on premium/authority uploads
- **Exact change:** For full courses, strategic reviews, and founder-facing authority videos, reduce thumbnail punctuation from `?!`, huge exclamation marks, and dramatic stickers to one clean claim or question.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** High-class authority reads confident. Too much urgency makes even smart content feel cheaper.
- **Quick implementation note:** Keep high-energy punctuation for breaking-news/tool-battle content only; use calm editorial typography for evergreen videos.
- **Priority:** Medium

## Today’s recommended 1% upgrade
**Draft a new banner right-side plaque to replace the red Subscribe/social block — no upload yet, just one approval-ready version.**

Why this is the best move today:
- It is a visible homepage-level upgrade that changes the channel’s luxury signal immediately without disrupting the working thumbnail/CTR system.
- It removes the loudest non-premium cue currently visible above the fold: the red subscribe graphic and crowded social icon strip.
- It aligns YouTube with the Midnight Luxe Editorial rule while preserving the existing authority proof: Jet portrait, Thai scale message, and Limitless Club identity.
