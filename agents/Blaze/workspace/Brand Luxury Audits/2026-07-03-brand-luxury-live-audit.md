- Rotation rationale: 2026-07-02 audit was Instagram, so today rotates to YouTube.

## First-impression read
Jet's YouTube channel immediately communicates scale and credibility: 106K subscribers, 1.3K videos, a polished dark portrait avatar, training-room social proof in the banner, and several recent AI education uploads with strong view velocity. The channel feels active, useful, and authoritative.

The main luxury gap is that the visible system still feels more like high-performance YouTube packaging than premium founder-media. The banner has strong proof, but its large red `SUBSCRIBE` button/click-hand graphic and social-icon strip add generic creator-platform energy. Recent thumbnails use many colors, glowing outlines, large yellow/orange type, reaction faces, token/discount badges, and mixed design languages. This helps clickability, but it dilutes the expensive Limitless Club association.

## Visible observations
- **Banner:** Strong proof collage with large Thai promise `คนไทย 1,000,000 คนต้องเก่ง AI`, Jet in black, and Limitless Club mark. Luxury drag comes from the red subscribe button, bell/cursor graphic, and several social icons competing with the message.
- **Avatar:** Excellent premium anchor: dark suit, dark background, direct eye contact, clean grooming, cinematic lighting. Keep as-is.
- **Homepage hierarchy:** Clear name, handle, subscriber proof, description, link, and tabs. The public first view is credible and easy to parse.
- **Recent thumbnail row:** Titles show a useful course/content ladder, but visual treatments vary: green/yellow worker thumbnail, blackboard/YouTube graphic, blue ChatGPT `$20`, orange `ปลดล็อค AI อีก 95%`, and orange token badge.
- **Brand memory:** The strongest luxury cues are Jet's black wardrobe, dark backgrounds, formal portrait, and the Limitless Club mark. The weakest cues are bright YouTube-native red/orange/yellow CTA treatments and inconsistent thumbnail title-strip systems.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Replace the banner's red subscribe module with a quiet Limitless Club proof seal
- **Exact change:** Draft a banner revision that keeps the Thai 1,000,000 AI mission, Jet portrait, and classroom proof, but removes the red `SUBSCRIBE` button, bell/cursor graphic, and social-icon strip. Replace with a small bronze/ivory seal: `LIMITLESS CLUB` / `AI Systems for Founders` / `@jeditrinupab`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Red subscribe graphics are platform-native and loud; a restraint-based seal makes the banner feel like a media brand masthead, not a creator callout.
- **Quick implementation note:** Design as a draft only. Keep safe-area readability for desktop/mobile/TV. Use `#17181A`, `#E2D7C8`, and rare `#94764A` lines.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Create a 3-family thumbnail system for future uploads
- **Exact change:** Sort thumbnails into three repeatable families: `Full Course` = charcoal/ivory title card with small Jet portrait; `Tools/Reviews` = dark product UI frame + controlled bronze tag; `Quick Wins/Tokens` = portrait still + one restrained numeric badge.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury channels feel like an editorial series library. Repetition creates brand memory and reduces the current rainbow effect.
- **Quick implementation note:** Do not re-cover everything. Start with the next 3 uploads and use the same footer: `LIMITLESS CLUB • @jeditrinupab`.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Standardize `[Full Course]` covers with one premium label treatment
- **Exact change:** For future `[Full Course]` videos, use one small top-left label: `FULL COURSE` in warm ivory on charcoal, with a thin bronze rule. Avoid large bracket text competing inside the YouTube title and cover.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** A consistent course-label system makes free content feel like a premium curriculum rather than scattered uploads.
- **Quick implementation note:** Keep `[Full Course]` in titles if it drives clarity/search; make the visual label calmer and more editorial.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Reduce emergency colors in thumbnails to one accent per video
- **Exact change:** For each new thumbnail, pick only one accent family: bronze OR AI green OR warning orange, not multiple bright accents together. Keep background dark or neutral.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Expensive brands use color sparingly. One controlled accent makes the message feel intentional instead of promotional.
- **Quick implementation note:** Apply especially to token/cost videos where orange/yellow currently dominates.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Add one calm channel-description line under the link CTA
- **Exact change:** Draft a description addition after the Line OA CTA: `Limitless Club: AI systems, content, and growth for serious founders.`
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The current description is action-first. A short editorial positioning line gives premium context before viewers judge the channel as only tutorial/content.
- **Quick implementation note:** Draft for Jet approval only; do not edit the channel profile live.
- **Priority:** Medium
