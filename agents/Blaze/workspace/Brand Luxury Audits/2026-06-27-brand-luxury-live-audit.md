# Brand Luxury 1% Live Audit — YouTube — 2026-06-27

## Scope
- Focus surface: YouTube `youtube.com/@jeditrinupab`
- Rotation rationale: 2026-06-26 audit was Instagram, so today rotates to YouTube.
- Live access: public logged-out YouTube channel loaded successfully. Inspected banner/avatar, homepage first impression, visible description/link area, tab row, recent upload row, section hierarchy, and visible posts.
- No public assets were edited, posted, scheduled, boosted, or changed.

## First-impression read
The channel has strong authority signals: 95.4K subscribers, 1.3K videos, clear AI/business topic ownership, a polished dark avatar, and an active recent upload row. The premium gap is visual restraint. Above the fold currently mixes a strong black banner with group-photo mosaics, a red subscribe graphic, multiple social icons, emoji/link-heavy description copy, and high-saturation thumbnails. This makes the channel feel energetic and educational, but less luxury founder-media.

## Visual observations
- **Banner:** The black center panel and Jet portrait are the strongest premium cues. The Thai mission line is clear and ambitious: `คนไทย 1,000,000 คนต้องเก่ง AI`. However, the banner also includes a red `SUBSCRIBE` button graphic, cursor/bell icon, social icons, and four group-photo panels. These elements add activity/proof but reduce high-class restraint.
- **Avatar:** Strong premium anchor. Dark studio-style portrait, black clothing, direct eye contact, clean contrast. It aligns well with Midnight Luxe Editorial.
- **Name and authority metadata:** `Jedi Trinupab`, 95.4K subscribers, and 1.3K videos read credible and established. The page has enough authority; it does not need loud visual proof everywhere.
- **Description/link preview:** The visible line leads with workshop booking and a Line OA link: `เช็ครอบอบรมกับทีมผม Line OA 👉 https://lin.ee/4v2Xsum ...more`. Useful for conversion, but the emoji and raw-link-first treatment feel more direct-response than premium.
- **Recent thumbnails:** First row uses varied saturated systems: blue/green, yellow/orange, lightning icons, large `%`, app screenshots, bright glows, expressive reaction faces. Good for CTR, but inconsistent as a premium storefront. The best luxury path is not removing energy; it is adding a consistent brand shell.
- **Homepage hierarchy:** Recent videos appear immediately, then Popular videos, AI playlist, Shorts, Posts. The content depth is strong, but there is no visible premium flagship shelf naming system such as `Limitless AI Brief`, `Founder AI Systems`, or `AI Operating System`.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) Remove the red subscribe-button graphic from the channel banner draft
- **Exact change:** Draft a cleaner banner version that keeps the black center panel, Jet portrait, Limitless Club mark, and Thai mission line, but removes the red `SUBSCRIBE` button, cursor, bell, and social icon row. Replace with one restrained footer line: `LIMITLESS CLUB | AI SYSTEMS FOR FOUNDERS` or `@jeditrinupab` in warm ivory.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury pages do not beg for attention; they signal confidence. Removing YouTube-red UI graphics instantly makes the banner feel more editorial, expensive, and controlled.
- **Quick implementation note:** Prepare as a draft only. Keep existing group-photo proof optional, but reduce it to one subtle monochrome strip or remove from desktop crop if the mission statement is the hero.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Create a thumbnail “premium shell” for strategic uploads
- **Exact change:** For flagship/non-news uploads, standardize a shell: charcoal border or top masthead, tiny `LIMITLESS CLUB`, warm ivory title block, one bronze accent, and consistent Jet portrait crop. Keep high-CTR objects/icons inside the frame, but avoid every thumbnail having a different color language.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The recent row currently feels like separate campaigns. A shared shell lets the channel retain YouTube performance while building brand memory and premium recognition.
- **Quick implementation note:** Start with the next 3 strategic videos only; do not retroactively redesign the whole archive. Suggested shell: 8–10% charcoal frame, ivory Thai headline, bronze micro-label such as `AI SYSTEMS`.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Draft a calmer above-fold description preview
- **Exact change:** Replace the visible preview with a more editorial line before the booking CTA, e.g. `Limitless Club — AI systems, growth strategy, and operator leverage for Thai founders.` Then second line: `อบรม/ร่วมงานกับทีม: Line OA [link]`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** The current first impression opens with a raw booking CTA and emoji. A premium channel should first define the worldview/category, then offer the conversion path.
- **Quick implementation note:** This is a live channel edit; keep as a draft for Jet approval.
- **Priority:** Medium-High

### 4) Rename homepage sections with a stronger editorial system
- **Exact change:** Where YouTube customization allows, use more branded section labels/playlists: `Start Here: AI Business Systems`, `Founder AI Playbooks`, `AI Tools Tested`, `Full Courses`, `Limitless Classroom`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Editorial naming makes the channel feel curated like a founder-media library, not just an upload archive.
- **Quick implementation note:** No need to change content. Reorganize or rename playlists/sections in YouTube Studio only after approval.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Add a proof system that is quieter than collage proof
- **Exact change:** Instead of multiple group photos in the banner, create a small proof line or badge: `95K+ subscribers • 1,000+ students • AI for business operators`. Use ivory text, bronze separator dots, no emoji.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Proof remains visible, but the channel stops looking like a training-sales page and starts looking like a premium authority platform.
- **Quick implementation note:** This can be combined with the cleaner banner draft. Keep the badge small; proof should support the mission line, not compete with it.
- **Priority:** Medium

## Today’s recommended 1% upgrade
**Draft a cleaner YouTube banner without the red subscribe UI graphic.**

Why this is the best move today:
- It upgrades the most visible brand asset above the fold.
- It keeps the winning black/portrait/mission foundation but removes the least luxury-coded element.
- It is low-risk: only a draft for approval, and it can be tested visually before any public change.
