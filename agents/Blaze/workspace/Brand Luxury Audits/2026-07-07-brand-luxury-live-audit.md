# Brand Luxury 1% Live Audit — YouTube — 2026-07-07

## Scope
- Focus surface: YouTube `youtube.com/@jeditrinupab`
- Rotation rationale: 2026-07-06 audit was Instagram, so today rotates to YouTube.
- Live access: Public YouTube channel loaded successfully in logged-out browser. Header, banner, avatar, navigation, recent video row, popular videos, AI playlist row, Shorts row, and Community posts were visible. No sign-in, profile edits, posting, scheduling, publishing, ads, payment, or permission dialogs were touched.

## First-impression read
Jet's YouTube channel has strong authority signals: 109K subscribers, 1.3K videos, clear founder name, professional portrait avatar, active recent uploads, multiple high-performing educational videos, and visible community posts. The account feels credible and alive.

The luxury gap is not authority; it is visual restraint and system consistency. The banner is high-energy proof-led, with event/group photos, a large Thai promise, Jet's image, Limitless Club mark, a red Subscribe button graphic, notification bell, social icons, and handle. The recent thumbnail row is optimized for click-through: large faces, bright text, yellow accents, app/tool graphics, arrows, X marks, question marks, and busy UI panels. This likely performs, but it slightly pulls the brand toward creator/YouTube tactics rather than expensive founder-media.

## Visible observations
- **Banner:** Strong proof and social-scale message: `คนไทย 1,000,000 คนต้องเก่ง AI`. Premium potential is high because Jet portrait + Limitless Club mark already exist, but the red subscribe graphic, bell, social icon strip, and multiple side collages create visual noise.
- **Avatar:** Excellent luxury cue. Dark portrait, controlled lighting, black styling, direct gaze, high confidence. It matches Midnight Luxe better than many page elements.
- **Header metadata:** Clean enough, but the public description first line starts with a direct training/Line OA CTA. Commercially useful, but the first impression could feel more institutional if the mission line came before the CTA.
- **Recent thumbnails:** High contrast and energetic. However, the first row mixes beige desk content, green sci-fi panels, app icons, yellow Thai text, multiple face crops, and English/Thai title systems. Strong for algorithmic attention; less strong for luxury memory.
- **Titles:** Recent titles vary from urgent news (`48 ชม.`), continuation phrasing, comparison, and `[Full Course]`. Good educational range, but recurring editorial labels could be tightened.
- **Homepage architecture:** Recent videos and popular videos show proof, but there is no immediately visible premium hero/trailer or curated “Start here” shelf above the latest upload row.

## Micro-upgrades

### 1) De-noise the YouTube banner by removing the fake subscribe/bell/social-icon cluster
- **Exact change:** In the banner artwork, keep Jet portrait, `Limitless Club`, the 1,000,000 Thai AI mission line, and 1–2 proof images max. Remove the red `SUBSCRIBE` button graphic, bell cursor, and social icon strip.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Luxury brands do not over-ask in the hero image. Removing platform-action graphics makes the banner feel more like a founder-media masthead and less like a YouTube growth template.
- **Quick implementation note:** Draft only for Jet approval. Use #17181A as base, warm ivory headline, one rare #94764A accent on `AI` or a thin divider. Keep the live YouTube Subscribe button doing the CTA job.
- **Priority:** High

### 2) Add a restrained banner metadata line under the mission
- **Exact change:** Add one small editorial line under the main Thai headline, e.g. `AI Systems • Founder Education • Limitless Club` or Thai equivalent `AI Systems สำหรับเจ้าของธุรกิจและทีม`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Premium media brands establish category clearly. A quiet metadata line makes the banner feel intentional and institution-level without adding clutter.
- **Quick implementation note:** Set in clean sans, small size, tracking slightly expanded, warm ivory at lower opacity. No icons.
- **Priority:** High

### 3) Create a two-tier thumbnail system: “News/Tool Alert” vs “Full Course/Authority”
- **Exact change:** Keep urgent, high-CTR thumbnails for AI news/tool videos, but give `[Full Course]` and pillar education videos a calmer template: charcoal base, one portrait or diagram, 3–5 word headline, small `Limitless Club` header, thin bronze rule, footer `@jeditrinupab`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Not every video should shout equally. Differentiating pillar assets makes the channel feel like a premium library, not only a daily feed.
- **Quick implementation note:** Start with future Full Course thumbnails only; no need to retrofit the whole library. Preserve face/hook but reduce app-logo clutter and yellow blocks.
- **Priority:** High

### 4) Tighten `[Full Course]` naming into an editorial series label
- **Exact change:** Keep `[Full Course]` for search clarity if it performs, but add a consistent subtitle pattern: `[Full Course] AI Agent Operating System — วิธีสร้างทีมด้วย AI` or `Limitless Course: AI Agent Operating System`.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Consistent series language makes the education catalog feel owned and premium. It also separates Jet's course assets from generic tutorial uploads.
- **Quick implementation note:** Draft naming rules before changing titles. Use on future uploads first; title edits on existing videos require Jet approval.
- **Priority:** Medium

### 5) Put a “Start Here”/pillar shelf above or near recent videos if available
- **Exact change:** Curate 3–5 strongest evergreen videos into a shelf such as `Start Here: Build With AI` or Thai equivalent, visually led by the best-performing AI Agent / AI OS / Personal Brand assets.
- **Why it increases luxury association:** Premium channels guide viewers. A curated shelf turns the homepage from chronological uploads into an intentional learning environment.
- **Quick implementation note:** This is a channel-layout draft for Jet approval only. Use existing top performers; no new content needed.
- **Priority:** Medium

## Today's recommended 1% upgrade
**Draft a cleaner YouTube banner v2 that removes the red Subscribe/bell/social icon cluster and turns the top of the channel into a restrained Limitless Club founder-media masthead.**

Why this is the best move today:
- The banner is the largest brand surface on the YouTube homepage and sets the luxury frame before thumbnails appear.
- It is a small asset-level change, not a rebrand or content strategy overhaul.
- It lets YouTube's native Subscribe button handle conversion while the banner handles status, mission, and brand memory.
