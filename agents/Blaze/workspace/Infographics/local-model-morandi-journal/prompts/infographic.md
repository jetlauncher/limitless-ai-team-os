## Layout Guidelines — dense-modules

High-density modular layout with 6 distinct information modules packed with concrete, useful guidance.
- Main title at top, prominent and readable.
- Use 6 rounded modular cards arranged in a clean magazine-like grid.
- Each module has a short Thai headline, 2–3 short labels, and a simple doodle.
- Include section markers such as MOD-01, MOD-02, etc.
- Dense but readable: avoid tiny Thai text.
- Every module should feel useful, not decorative.

## Style Guidelines — morandi-journal

Warm hand-drawn bullet journal aesthetic:
- Background: warm cream/beige paper texture (#F5F0E6)
- Primary: muted teal/sage green (#7BA3A8) for headers and frames
- Secondary: warm terracotta/orange (#D4956A) for highlights and numbers
- Line art: dark charcoal brown (#4A4540)
- Soft highlights: pale yellow (#F5E6C8)
- Hand-drawn doodle illustrations with organic, slightly imperfect ink lines
- Washi tape strips, rounded cards, dotted frames, connecting arrows, tiny stars/clouds/sparkles
- Main title: bold hand-lettered Thai style, readable
- Module headers: clean handwritten Thai text on dark teal rounded badges
- Body text: neat handwritten Thai print style, large enough to read

Avoid:
- Corporate flat vector look
- Dark cyberpunk style
- Tiny unreadable Thai
- Random English clutter
- Incorrect Thai spelling
