## Strategic rationale

I picked this video because it has the strongest carousel ingredients:

- Concrete proof: 150 days of real AI Agent usage.
- Founder/operator relevance: small team, real business, real workflows.
- Clear market implication: AI Agent is not a tool; it is a new team structure.
- Strong CTA fit: leads naturally into AI Agent / AI Operating System programs.
