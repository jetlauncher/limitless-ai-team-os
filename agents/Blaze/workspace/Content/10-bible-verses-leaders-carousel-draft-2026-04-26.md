### Slide 2 — Proverbs 3:13–18

**1. Wisdom creates better returns than gold.**

“Blessed are those who find wisdom, those who gain understanding…”

Leadership lesson:  
Before chasing more money, ask for more wisdom. Wisdom protects the decisions money cannot fix.

TH: สติปัญญามีค่าล้ำกว่าทองคำ

---

### Slide 3 — 2 Corinthians 5:17

**2. You are not trapped by your old identity.**

“If anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!”

Leadership lesson:  
Growth requires leaving old patterns behind. God can renew not only your heart, but also the way you lead.

TH: สิ่งเก่าๆ ล่วงไป สิ่งใหม่เริ่มต้นแล้ว

---

### Slide 4 — Psalm 23:6

**3. Goodness and mercy can follow you into your work.**

“Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life…”

Leadership lesson:  
You do not build alone. God’s goodness is not only for Sunday — it follows you into decisions, pressure, and responsibility.

TH: ความดีและความเมตตาจะติดตามข้าพเจ้า

---

### Slide 5 — Psalm 34:8

**4. Test God’s goodness for yourself.**

“Oh, taste and see that the Lord is good! Blessed is the man who takes refuge in him!”

Leadership lesson:  
Faith is not just theory. In seasons of uncertainty, refuge in God becomes real through experience.

TH: จงลิ้มรสและรู้ว่าพระองค์ประเสริฐ

---

### Slide 6 — Psalm 100:5

**5. God’s faithfulness outlasts every season.**

“For the Lord is good; his steadfast love endures forever…”

Leadership lesson:  
Markets change. Plans change. People change. God’s faithfulness does not.

TH: ความรักมั่นคงของพระองค์ดำรงอยู่ตลอดกาล

---

### Slide 7 — Ephesians 2:8–9

**6. Grace keeps ambition humble.**

“By grace you have been saved through faith… it is the gift of God, not a result of works…”

Leadership lesson:  
Work hard, but do not worship your work. The deepest things in life are received, not achieved.

TH: ความรอดเป็นของขวัญจากพระเจ้า

---

### Slide 8 — Titus 3:4–5

**7. Mercy, not ego, is the foundation.**

“He saved us, not because of works done by us in righteousness, but according to his own mercy…”

Leadership lesson:  
The strongest leaders remember they are recipients of mercy before they are producers of results.

TH: พระองค์ช่วยเราเพราะความเมตตา

---

### Slide 9 — Romans 8:28

**8. God can work through every part of the story.**

“In all things God works for the good of those who love him…”

Leadership lesson:  
Even setbacks can become material for purpose. Nothing is wasted when God is involved.

TH: พระเจ้าทรงให้ทุกสิ่งร่วมกันเกิดผลดี

---

### Slide 10 — James 1:17

**9. Every good gift has a source.**

“Every good gift and every perfect gift is from above…”

Leadership lesson:  
Talent, opportunity, timing, relationships, resources — treat them as stewardship, not entitlement.

TH: สิ่งดีทุกอย่างมาจากเบื้องบน

---

### Slide 11 — Proverbs 10:4

**10. Diligence is spiritual and practical.**

“Lazy hands make for poverty, but diligent hands bring wealth.”

Leadership lesson:  
Faith does not excuse laziness. God honors diligence, discipline, and faithful work.

TH: มือของคนขยันนำความมั่งมีมาสู่ตน
