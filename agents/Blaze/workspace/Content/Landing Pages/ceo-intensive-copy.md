## What is weak in the current live copy

The current page has strong components but reads like a workshop menu:

- Hero promise: "Stop being the bottleneck" is good, but the page does not show enough of the before/after operational reality.
- Format contradiction: the page says "in one day", "2 Days", "9 Hours", and "Full Day Workshop" in different places. This needs one clear answer.
- The body is tool-led: prompts, CustomGPT, Airtable, Notion, email automation. Searchers looking for a CEO Intensive membership program need to see transformation, qualification, and decision value first.
- Proof exists on the broader site, but this page needs CEO-specific proof or at least a proof block that points to founder/executive outcomes.
- CTA should feel like qualification, not checkout. "Apply Now" is better than "Reserve your spot", but the body should explain who gets accepted.

### What you build

H2:
The six systems you build inside CEO OS Intensive

1. Master prompt library

Your first 10-15 prompts for the questions and decisions your business repeats: customer replies, team assignments, follow-up messages, meeting summaries, decision framing, and founder-level thinking.

2. Chained prompt workflows

Step-by-step workflows for repeated work such as client onboarding, content planning, customer communication, and internal reporting. The point is to make the process reusable, not dependent on someone remembering how to prompt.

3. CustomGPT suite

Three AI assistants shaped around your company context. The current live page names these as Operations Commander, Strategic Advisor, and one custom role based on your business.

4. CEO dashboard

A working Airtable dashboard for projects, owners, status, next actions, and pipeline visibility. The CEO should be able to see what needs attention without asking five people for updates.

5. Company wiki

A Notion knowledge base for SOPs, onboarding notes, FAQs, templates, and repeated decisions. This becomes the place your team checks before asking the founder.

6. Email and follow-up templates

Reusable templates for welcome sequences, client follow-ups, internal reminders, and common communication flows, so follow-up does not depend on memory or mood.

### FAQ section

H2:
FAQ

#### Is CEO Intensive the same as CEO OS Intensive?

Pending Jet confirmation. Recommended positioning: CEO Intensive is the search-friendly category and CEO OS Intensive is the branded Limitless Club program.

#### Is this a beginner AI class?

No. You do not need to be technical, but you should be ready to apply AI to real business operations. If you want a broader beginner-friendly AI class, Shortcut to AI Expert may be the better starting point.

#### Do I need a team to join?

You do not need a large team, but the program works best when you already have repeated work, customer communication, projects, or decisions that need to move without the founder answering everything manually.

#### What should I bring?

Bring a laptop, ChatGPT Plus, Airtable, Notion, and real business material: SOPs, common customer questions, team workflows, sales process notes, project tracking examples, or anything your team keeps asking you about.

#### Will Jet or the team build everything for me?

No. This is guided implementation. You build with support so you understand the system and can keep improving it after the workshop.
