## Original claim

> I handed my client’s Instagram over to Claude and let it run the account by itself.
>
> The result? 3.7 million views in 18 days on a profile that was created less than 30 days ago.
>
> Copy the 5 prompts I used:

## Prompt 1 — Audience Research Engine

```text
Analyze my niche: [niche] and audience: [audience]. Identify their biggest frustrations, goals, fears, objections, desires, and questions. Then explain exactly what type of content they are most likely to watch, save, share, comment on, and follow.
```

## Prompt 2 — Content Opportunity Finder

```text
Generate 50 Instagram content ideas for [niche] targeting [audience]. Focus on audience mistakes, myths, lessons, opinions, trends, hidden opportunities, and practical advice. Prioritize ideas with the highest potential for reach, engagement, and follower growth.
```

## Prompt 3 — Hook and Script Builder

```text
Take this content idea: [paste idea]. Create 20 hooks and a complete reel script. Make the hooks attention grabbing, the script easy to follow, and the content valuable enough to maximize watch time, saves, and shares.
```

## Prompt 4 — Content Multiplication Engine

```text
Take this content: [paste content]. Generate 10 new content pieces from it. Create new hooks, angles, examples, stories, carousel concepts, reel ideas, and follow up posts. Make every version feel original while keeping the core message intact.
```

## Prompt 5 — Instagram Growth System

```text
Act as my Instagram strategist. Using my niche: [niche], audience: [audience], goals: [goals], and recent content: [paste 5 posts], create a complete Instagram growth system. Include content planning, topic generation, hook creation, scripting, repurposing, posting priorities, and engagement tactics. Design the system to consistently grow views, followers, and reach without daily brainstorming.
```

## Blaze note

These are useful as a simple public-facing “AI content OS” prompt stack, but the actual growth claim is unverified. The stronger Limitless Club interpretation is: prompts are not the system; the real advantage is a repeatable content operating system with positioning, QA, publishing cadence, and feedback loops.
