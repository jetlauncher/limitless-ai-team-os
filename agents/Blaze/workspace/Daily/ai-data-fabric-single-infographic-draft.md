## Central visual

Center: “AI Output Quality”
Small label underneath: “Not only a model problem.”

Around it: 4 connected layers that determine whether AI creates value.

## Layer 1 — Data Quality

Question:
Is your business data clean enough to trust?

Signals of weakness:
• duplicate records
• outdated data
• missing fields
• inconsistent naming

## Layer 2 — System Integration

Question:
Can AI access the right systems together?

Signals of weakness:
• CRM, finance, ops, support tools are disconnected
• manual copy-paste between tools
• no single source of truth

## Layer 3 — Business Context

Question:
Does AI understand how decisions are made?

Signals of weakness:
• process knowledge lives in people’s heads
• teams define metrics differently
• unclear rules and exceptions

## Layer 4 — Governance & Access

Question:
Can AI use data safely and correctly?

Signals of weakness:
• unclear data ownership
• weak permissions
• sensitive data exposed
• no audit trail

## Bottom section — Founder checklist

Before AI training, audit the AI-readiness layer:

✓ Where does important data live?
✓ Which systems are disconnected?
✓ Who owns data quality?
✓ Do teams use the same definitions?
✓ What context does AI need?
✓ What should AI never touch?

## Closing line

The next AI advantage will not only come from better models.
It will come from cleaner operations and better data plumbing.
