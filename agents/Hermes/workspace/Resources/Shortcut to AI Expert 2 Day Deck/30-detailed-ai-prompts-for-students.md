# 30 Detailed AI Prompts for Students

Use these as examples of **clear prompting**. Each prompt gives AI enough context, role, goal, constraints, output format, and quality standard.

## How to Use These Prompts

Replace the placeholders in brackets:

- `[business]` = your business or project
- `[customer]` = your target customer
- `[offer]` = your product/service
- `[goal]` = what you want to achieve
- `[tone]` = professional, friendly, premium, direct, Thai casual, etc.
- `[platform]` = Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LINE, YouTube, email, etc.

## The 7-Part Clear Prompt Formula

A strong prompt usually includes:

1. **Role** — who the AI should act as
2. **Context** — your business/customer/situation
3. **Goal** — what you want to achieve
4. **Input** — information the AI should use
5. **Constraints** — rules, tone, length, do/don't
6. **Output format** — bullets, table, checklist, script, SOP, etc.
7. **Quality bar** — what “good” means

Template:

```text
Act as [expert role].

Context:
[describe business, audience, situation]

Goal:
[describe desired outcome]

Use this input:
[paste notes, data, transcript, offer, customer info]

Constraints:
- [tone]
- [length]
- [do/don't]
- [language]

Output format:
[exact format you want]

Before answering, think through the problem step by step. If anything is unclear, make reasonable assumptions and state them briefly.
```

# A. Business Strategy Prompts

## 1. Business Clarity Prompt

**Use when:** Students are not clear on what their business really does or who it serves.

```text
Act as a business strategist for small business owners.

Context:
I run [business]. We sell [offer/product/service] to [customer type]. Right now, I feel unclear about how to explain what we do in a simple and compelling way.

Goal:
Help me clarify my business in plain language so I can explain it to customers, partners, and AI tools.

Use this input:
- Business name: [name]
- Main offer: [offer]
- Target customer: [customer]
- Main problem we solve: [problem]
- Main result customers want: [result]
- Current tagline or explanation: [paste if any]

Output format:
1. One-sentence business description
2. Simple “We help X do Y without Z” statement
3. 3 alternative positioning angles
4. 5 words customers should associate with our brand
5. A sharper 30-second pitch

Constraints:
- Use clear, simple language
- Avoid jargon
- Make it sound premium but still human
- If information is missing, state your assumptions
```

# Student Exercise

Ask students to pick one prompt and customize it with their own business details. Then ask AI:

```text
Before you answer, rate my prompt from 1-10 and tell me what information is missing. Then ask me the 3 most important follow-up questions before producing the final output.
```

This teaches students that good AI work is not about one perfect magic prompt. It is about giving context, improving instructions, and working with AI like a capable teammate.
