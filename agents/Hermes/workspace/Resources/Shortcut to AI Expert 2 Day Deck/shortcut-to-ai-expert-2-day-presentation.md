# Business Context Profile

The profile should help AI understand:

- Who the business serves
- What the offer is
- Customer pain points
- Brand voice
- FAQs and objections
- Current goals
- Important constraints
- Examples of good output

If the profile is vague, AI output will be vague.

# Block 5
## AI Agents and Role-Based Workflows

**Big idea:** Design your AI team before you automate anything.

Participants build:

- AI Team Org Chart
- 3 AI role briefs
- One working agent-style workflow

Example AI roles:

- Sales assistant
- Research assistant
- Content strategist
- Customer support assistant
- Finance/admin assistant
- Learning coach

---

# Agent Brief Template

Every AI role needs:

- Mission
- Inputs
- Outputs
- Tone
- Tools
- Boundaries
- Success criteria
- Human approval checkpoint

An agent is not just a prompt.

An agent is a repeatable role inside a workflow.

---

# Block 6
## Automation + SOPs + Human-in-the-Loop

**Big idea:** Automate repeatable work, not unclear thinking.

Participants build:

- Automation Opportunity Scorecard
- One complete SOP
- One approval flow

Automation ladder:

1. Manual with AI help
2. Checklist
3. Template
4. Semi-automated workflow
5. Full automation with monitoring

---

# Human-in-the-Loop Design

For each workflow, participants decide:

- What can AI draft?
- What must a human approve?
- What should be rejected?
- What should be escalated?
- What happens if AI is wrong?

Safe automation creates trust.

Unsafe automation creates cleanup work.

---

# Block 7
## Decision Support, Research, and Evaluation

**Big idea:** AI should help you think better, not just produce more.

Participants build:

- AI research brief
- One-page decision memo
- Output evaluation checklist

They practice:

- Asking better questions
- Separating evidence from opinion
- Comparing tradeoffs
- Identifying risks
- Making a next-step decision

---

# Evaluation Checklist

Every AI output should be reviewed for:

- Accuracy
- Usefulness
- Specificity
- Actionability
- Brand fit
- Risk
- Source quality
- Human judgment required

The goal is not blind trust.

The goal is better thinking with AI support.
