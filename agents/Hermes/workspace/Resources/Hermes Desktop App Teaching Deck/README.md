Audience is non-technical business owners. The deck focuses on practical business leverage: research, drafting, visual analysis, SOPs, sales follow-up, recurring automations, memory, skills, and safe approval boundaries.

## Safety framing
Core classroom mantra: “Draft automatically. Publish intentionally.” Students are encouraged to use Hermes for internal drafts and analysis, while keeping human approval for spending, posting, customer messaging, deleting files, and live-system changes.
