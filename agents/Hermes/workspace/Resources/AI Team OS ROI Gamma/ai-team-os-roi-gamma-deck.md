# Fully Loaded Cost

Base salary is not the full cost.

Add approximately 25% for:

- recruiting
- benefits
- taxes/admin
- software/tools
- management time
- replacement risk
- coordination drag

**Estimated fully loaded cost:**  
฿1.006M–฿2.075M/month

**Annualized:**  
฿12.075M–฿24.9M/year

# AI Tooling Cost Assumption

A serious AI operating stack may cost roughly:

**฿20K–฿80K/month**
