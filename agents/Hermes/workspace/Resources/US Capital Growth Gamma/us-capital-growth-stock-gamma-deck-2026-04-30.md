# Sector Thesis — AI Semiconductors

- **AMD:** highest catch-up torque if AI accelerator roadmap converts into revenue.
- **NVDA:** best-in-class AI leader; still strongest data metrics, but expectations are high.
- **AVGO:** most attractive risk-adjusted infrastructure angle: networking, custom silicon, VMware/software.
- Key watch item: hyperscaler AI capex durability and China/export restrictions.

---

# Sector Thesis — Energy and Power

- **LNG:** long-cycle energy infrastructure and global security beneficiary.
- **DVN:** more cyclical FCF/value angle; needs fresh valuation and oil-price sensitivity check.
- **FSLR / VST / CEG / NEE:** power-demand and electrification exposure.
- Key watch item: rates, regulation, commodity prices, and policy shifts.

---

# Sector Thesis — Healthcare

- **LLY:** strongest growth engine in healthcare; obesity/metabolic mega-cycle.
- **BSX:** medtech growth and procedure recovery; strong upside in current data.
- **ISRG:** robotic surgery platform compounder.
- **JNJ:** defensive low-beta pharma/medtech anchor; capital gains rely on pipeline execution and re-rating.

---

# Sector Thesis — Banks

- **C / FITB:** re-rating and turnaround upside, but higher execution/macro risk.
- **JPM:** best quality anchor; less upside than re-rating candidates.
- **MS / GS / SCHW / BK:** capital markets and platform leverage.
- Key watch item: net interest income compression if Fed cuts faster than expected.

---

# Key Risks to the Thesis

- **Multiple compression:** growth names hurt most if market P/E de-rates.
- **AI volatility:** semiconductor upside depends on continued capex and no severe export reversal.
- **Banks:** NII compression, credit losses, and yield curve shifts.
- **Healthcare:** drug pricing and policy risk.
- **Energy:** commodity volatility, rates, and political/regulatory shifts.
