```text
AI is not the strategy.

The strategy is deciding where intelligence should sit inside the business.

Most teams start with tools. The better starting point is workflow.

Ask:

• What signals do we need to notice faster?
• What decisions repeat every week?
• What information is trapped in chat or memory?
• What should be drafted by AI but approved by a human?
• What should trigger an alert instead of a meeting?

Once those answers are clear, the tools become obvious.

The goal is not to “use more AI.”

The goal is to build a business where the next action is easier to see.
```
