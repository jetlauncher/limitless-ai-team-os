### 2026-05-22 10:00 ICT — linkedin — 2026-05-22 LinkedIn — Build AI workflows, not AI habits

Most founders are still using AI as a personal habit.

A better move is to turn one repeated job into a company workflow.

Old way:
• Ask AI when you remember
• Keep the prompt in one person’s head
• Let every team member get a different answer
• Measure progress by how impressive the output feels

New way:
• Pick one repeated job
• Write a one-page business brief
• Use a simple 10-80-10 workflow
• Keep examples, source files, approval rules, and final SOPs outside the tool
• Measure time saved, error reduced, or decision speed

For Thai SMEs, this matters because knowledge often lives inside a few senior people.

If AI stays in one person’s laptop, the business does not get stronger.

If AI becomes a workflow the team can repeat, the company gains an operating layer.

This week, do not try to “use more AI.”

Choose one job your team repeats every week and turn it into a written AI workflow.
