```text
Google AI Search is a warning for business owners.

The customer journey is changing from:

Search → Website → Read → Ask the team

to:

Ask AI → Get an answer → Decide faster

That means your company needs more than content. It needs a source of truth that AI, your team, and your customers can trust.

This week, I would start with one simple document:

• What we sell
• Who it is for
• Pricing and conditions
• FAQ
• Proof and customer cases
• What AI must never invent
• When a human must review the answer

For a Thai SME, this is not a technical project. It is an operating discipline project.

The companies that win will not be the ones using the most tools. They will be the ones with the clearest workflows, cleanest knowledge base, and fastest review rhythm.
```
