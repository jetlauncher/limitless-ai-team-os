```
The fastest way to make AI useful inside a small business is not another prompt pack.

It is a one-page operating policy.

Every team should know five things:

• what data can go into AI tools
• what data must stay out
• which tasks AI can draft
• which decisions require human approval
• where good prompts and examples are stored

Without this, AI usage becomes random. One person has a good workflow. Another person creates risk. The founder still has to review everything manually.

With a simple policy, AI becomes a company asset instead of a private habit.

This week, pick one workflow such as sales replies, meeting summaries, weekly reporting, or SOP writing.

Then document the rule: human sets context, AI drafts, human approves.

That small system is where serious AI adoption starts.
```
