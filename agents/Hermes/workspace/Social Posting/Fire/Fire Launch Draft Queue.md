## Fire linkedin draft D1 — 2026-05-01T10:00:00
Platform: linkedin
Status: ready_for_review

AI is not the advantage by itself.

The advantage is the operating system around it.

Most teams are still using AI like a smarter search box: open a chat, ask a question, copy the answer, repeat. Useful, but not transformative.

The real leverage comes when AI is wired into the business rhythm:

• what changed today
• what requires attention
• what should be drafted
• what should be escalated
• what should be measured

That is the shift Fire is built around: AI as an operating layer, not a novelty tool.
