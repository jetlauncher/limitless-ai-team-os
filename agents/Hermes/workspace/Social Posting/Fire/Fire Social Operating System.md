# Fire — Social Operating System

## Goal
Make Fire feel alive every day without turning it into noisy spam.

## Default cadence after approval
- X: 3 short authority posts/day
- LinkedIn: 1 deeper founder/operator post/day
- Threads: 2–3 punchy mobile-first posts/day
- Instagram: 2–3 carousels/week once visuals are approved

## Content pillars
1. AI Team OS — replacing scattered tools with operating rhythms.
2. Founder leverage — less busywork, more decision clarity.
3. Workflow design — prompts are not the moat; systems are.
4. Revenue visibility — making money/events emotionally visible.
5. Case notes — “before/after” examples from actual builds.
6. Belief shift — AI as management layer, not just assistant.

## Voice rules
- Crisp, confident, founder-to-founder.
- No generic AI hype.
- Short hooks, strong mental models, practical workflows.
- Premium but direct.
- Use Thai only when targeting Thai Threads/IG audience; otherwise English by default.

## Approval workflow
1. Kelly drafts into local queue.
2. Jet approves/rejects/edits.
3. Kelly schedules through Blotato after approval.
4. Kelly monitors published URLs and failures.
5. Weekly review: keep winners, kill weak themes.
