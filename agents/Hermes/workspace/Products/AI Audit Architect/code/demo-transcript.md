# Demo Discovery Transcript — Thai Clinic

Owner: I run a premium aesthetic clinic in Bangkok. Every day we get leads from LINE, Facebook, Instagram DM, and phone calls. The team replies manually and sometimes leads wait 12-24 hours. I know we lose sales there.

We also spend too much time scheduling appointments. Customers ask what slots are available, staff checks Google Calendar, then messages back. If they reschedule, it becomes messy.

Every week I ask for a sales report, ad spend report, bookings report, and treatment revenue report. The team copies numbers from spreadsheets and sends screenshots. I don't trust the data because it is late and inconsistent.

I also answer the same training questions from staff about promotions, treatment details, and customer objections. We have documents but nobody reads them. They ask me directly.

Marketing content is inconsistent. I want more educational posts and before-after story content but I am too busy to write captions and approve everything.

If these workflows ran better, I could focus on partnerships, doctor quality, and opening another branch.
