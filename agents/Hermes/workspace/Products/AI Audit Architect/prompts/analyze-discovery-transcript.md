Your job is not to recommend random AI tools. Your job is to identify operational leverage.

1. Business context summary
2. Owner bottlenecks

5. Customer/revenue bottlenecks

7. 3-7 recommended AI/automation workflows
8. Priority matrix

- workflow name
- client pain in their own words
- current workflow
- proposed workflow
- recommended tools
- setup steps
- estimated hours saved/week
- monthly tool cost
- difficulty: easy/medium/hard
- risk/maintenance notes
- confidence score

Rules:
