## Executive verdict

The campaign has a strong premium black/gold workshop identity and credible founder/AI education feel. It does **not need a full redesign**, but it does need **performance cleanup** before using heavily in paid social.

Main issue: many slides look polished in a design preview but are not aggressive enough for mobile feed performance. The headlines and CTAs need to be larger, simpler, and more consistent.

## Overall strengths

- Strong brand consistency: black, gold, white, grayscale workshop photography.
- Premium and credible, not cheap or template-like.
- Good topic-market fit: AI Agent, no-code, business workflow, founder/operator leverage.
- Presenter photos create trust, especially when face/gesture is clear.
- Several strong message angles already exist.

## Overall weaknesses

- Mobile readability is inconsistent; subcopy is often too small.
- CTA styling is inconsistent: gold, black, pale gray, and footer bars vary too much.
- Some ads are too dark or low-contrast.
- Some photos are too small, dim, or emotionally flat.
- Several slides use generic or abstract AI language instead of tangible outcomes.
- Originals are cohesive but less thumb-stopping than the best alternatives.

## Main improvement rules

### 1. Mobile-first typography

- Increase headline size.
- Remove 30–50% of body text.
- Make sure the main idea is readable at thumbnail size.

### 2. One slide, one idea

Avoid cramming headline, body, microcopy, diagram, tool names, CTA, and photo into one card. Each slide should have one job.

### 3. CTA consistency

Use one primary CTA system across the set.

Recommended: gold CTA bar/button.

Possible CTA text:

- “จองที่นั่ง Workshop”
- “ดูรายละเอียด Workshop”
- “เริ่มสร้าง AI Agent แรก”
- “สมัครเรียนสด 1 วัน”

### 4. Use stronger human photos

Prioritize photos where:

- face is clear
- gesture is expressive
- presenter feels energetic and trustworthy
- image is bright enough to read in-feed

Best examples: `v14`, `v19`, `v27`, `v30`.

### 5. Make outcomes concrete

Stronger than generic “AI Agent” language:

- “สร้าง AI Agent ตัวแรกใน 1 วัน”
- “เปลี่ยน prompt ให้เป็น workflow”
- “ลดงานซ้ำ ๆ ในธุรกิจ”
- “ทำให้ทีมเร็วขึ้นโดยไม่ต้องเพิ่มคน”
- “เลิกใช้ AI แค่ถาม-ตอบ ให้มันช่วยทำงานจริง”
