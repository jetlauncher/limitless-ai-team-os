# Source picked for Run 01

From Plaud content batch P3: “อย่าให้ AI ทำ 100% กฎ 10-80-10”

Core transcript idea:
- If you let AI do 100%, you damage your brand.
- If you do everything yourself, you cannot scale.
- Use 10-80-10:
  - 10% first: your idea, judgment, taste, brand DNA.
  - 80% middle: AI carries the heavy production work.
  - 10% final: human audits what works / does not work.
- Proof example: 25 years of experience is unfair advantage that AI cannot copy.
