Thai business owners should prioritize agent tools that support AGENTS.md compatibility. The ecosystem is consolidating around one layer — choosing wrong means vendor lock-in.
- → **Best Jet angle:** "The AI Agent Standard Nobody Talked About." Explain AGENTS.md in Thai for non-technical founders: it's like making sure your workers can all speak the same language, so they collaborate seamlessly instead of each building their own siloed tool.
