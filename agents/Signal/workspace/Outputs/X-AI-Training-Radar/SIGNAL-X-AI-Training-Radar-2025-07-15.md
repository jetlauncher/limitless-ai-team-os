### 2. Synthetic Data Training Is Becoming a Real Discipline — "Accumulate, Don't Replace"
Peer-reviewed evidence (Nature 2024) confirms model collapse is real but avoidable if synthetic data accumulates alongside real data. Microsoft's Phi-1 proved a 1.3B model trained on curated synthetic data can match models 10× its size on coding benchmarks. Cosmopedia (HuggingFace) generated 25B tokens with <1% duplicates. Four distinct use cases now have separate decision frameworks: fine-tuning data, eval sets, edge-case augmentation, privacy substitution.
