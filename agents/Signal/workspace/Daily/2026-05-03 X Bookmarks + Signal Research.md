- Core modules:
  1. Map recurring workflows.
  2. Build a company brain/context base.

### Founders

- AI audits should evolve from “which tools should I buy?” into **production readiness audits**: model agility, evals, permissions, account security, migration plan, and ROI dashboard.
- AWS/Google/OpenAI signals show enterprise buyers will care about migration, governance, and data workflows as much as model novelty.

### Operators

- “Vibe coding” is becoming operator literacy: non-engineers can create internal tools, but need specs, context files, test cases, deployment hygiene, and rollback.
- The practical skill is translating business process into an AI-executable workflow.

### Educators

- Teach students and teams how to work with AI as a co-worker, not just a tutor: define tasks, inspect outputs, evaluate quality, and prevent over-reliance/sycophancy.
- Anthropic’s guidance research is a strong source for a module on AI advisor limits.
