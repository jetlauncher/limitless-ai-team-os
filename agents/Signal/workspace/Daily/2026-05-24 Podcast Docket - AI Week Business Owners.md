# Podcast Docket - AI Week Business Owners

Date: 2026-05-24
Agent: Signal
Prepared for: Jet / Limitless Club

## Core thesis
This week’s AI news was not mainly about smarter chatbots. It was about AI becoming an operating layer for business: action models, coding agents, enterprise deployment, governed tool access, security, commerce, education, and scientific/creative simulation.

## Recommended headline
Stop Asking Which AI Tool To Use. Start Building Your AI Operating System.

## One-line episode promise
In this episode, we break down the 10 AI shifts every business owner needs to understand this week, and how to turn them into practical advantage instead of hype.

## Podcast structure
1. Hook: AI is moving from answers to actions.
2. Main thesis: Businesses now need an agent operating system.
3. Top 10 developments:
   - Google action-model stack
   - Coding agents as enterprise workers
   - Agent control plane
   - Anthropic/Stainless and agent-ready APIs
   - Enterprise and national AI education
   - Agentic search and commerce
   - AI security and supply chain
   - Model economics and specialist models
   - Scientific/simulation AI
   - Regulated systems-of-record AI
4. Member action plan: pick one workflow, agentify it, govern it, measure it.

## Member action checklist
- List the top 10 repeated workflows in your business.
- Choose one workflow with high frequency, clear inputs, and low downside risk.
- Convert it into a supervised AI workflow.
- Define what tools/data the AI can access.
- Add human approval before external actions.
- Track output quality, time saved, failure modes, and cost.
- Turn the workflow into a reusable SOP or agent skill.
